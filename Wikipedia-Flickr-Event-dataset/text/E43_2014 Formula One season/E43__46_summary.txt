Talks between the current management and administrators overseeing F1 team supplier Caterham Sports Limited (CSL) are set to continue on Thursday.
FERNANDES DISPUTECaterham's current management team issued a strongly worded statement on Wednesday making it clear that they could turn their back on the team amid frustrations about the actions of former owner Tony Fernandes.
"Since their appointment, the administrators have released various press statements, which have been severely detrimental to the management of the Caterham F1 team," said the statement.
"After three months of operating the team in good faith, the buyer is now forced to explore all its options including the withdrawal of its management team."
Under the terms of contracts with Formula One Management, teams must compete in each championship event to gain commercial rights income.
