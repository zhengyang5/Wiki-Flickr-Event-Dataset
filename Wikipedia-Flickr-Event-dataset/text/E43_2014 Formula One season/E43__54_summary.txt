World Motor Sport CouncilA meeting of the FIA World Motor Sport Council (WMSC) took place today (27 September) in Dubrovnik, Croatia.
It is clarified that the hand-cutting of tyres is forbidden in the events of the FIA European Rally Cup, as well as the FIA European Rally Championship.
The format of the European Championship for the flagship category, SuperCar, will be adapted and concentrated over five of the 10 European events in the World Championship.
This will also have a specific classification for non-permanent drivers not entered in the entirety of the World Championship.
MOTOR SPORT TASK FORCEThe Motor Sport Task Force will nominate its members after the FIA Presidential Election.
