By SAMIR HOSNY and AHMED ABDEL RAHMANCAIRO: The “Revolution Candidate” campaign will begin collecting signatures and promoting its presidential candidate Hamdeen Sabbahi, the movement’s coordinator Amr Badr told CBC Channel on Saturday night.
This comes after Popular Current founder and former presidential candidate Sabbahi announced running in the upcoming presidential elections in a press conference earlier on Saturday.
Two members of the Tamarod movement are also supporting Sabbahi’s presidential candidacy, they stated that during the conference which was attended by a large number of young people, Badr added.
No other candidate has officially announced his intention to run for president, people are still awaiting the decision of Minister of Defense Abdel Fatah al-Sisi, who received wide public support for his presidential candidacy.
