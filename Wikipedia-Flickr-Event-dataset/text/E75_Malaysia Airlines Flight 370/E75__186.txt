The co-pilot of the missing Malaysian plane's last words heard from the cockpit were 'all right, good night', according to the airline's chief executive. Malaysia Airlines' Ahmad Jauhari Yahya tells a news conference on Monday it was unclear exactly when one of the plane's automatic tracking systems, known as ACARS, had been disabled. CCTV footage has emerged of the pilot and co-pilot going through security before the plane's take-off
