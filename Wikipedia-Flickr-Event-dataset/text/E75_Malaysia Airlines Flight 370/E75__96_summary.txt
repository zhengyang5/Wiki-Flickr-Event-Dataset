This operational report has been developed to provide regular updates on the progress of the search effort for MH370.
Key developments this weekOn 21 January, GO Phoenix recommenced search operations following the suspension caused by ex-Tropical Cyclone Bansi.
Fugro Equator and Fugro Discovery continue underwater search operations.
Assuming no other significant delays with vessels, equipment or from the weather, the current underwater search area may be largely completed around May 2015.
