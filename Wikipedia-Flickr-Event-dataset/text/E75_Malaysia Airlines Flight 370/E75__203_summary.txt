Mainland travel agencies have reported a sharp drop in the number of Chinese visitors to Malaysia.

“The impact has spread to most destinations in Southeast Asia,” said a travel agent with the China International who declined to be named.
“We might see the real impact in one or two months.”According to Tourism Malaysia, 1.79 million Chinese visited the country last year, a nearly 15 per cent annual increase.
China Youth Travel Service has stopped collaborating with Malaysia Airlines on package tours in Kota Kinabalu and promised to a full refund to any customers who wanted to cancel.
