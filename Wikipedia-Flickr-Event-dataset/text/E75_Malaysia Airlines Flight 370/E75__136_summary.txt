The government confirms that search and rescue efforts at both corridors have started for the missing MH370.
VIDEO INSIDESEPANG: The government today confirmed that search and rescue operations for the missing Malaysia Airlines MH370 in the northern and southern corridors have started.
This includes two groups: first, countries in the search corridors; and second, countries from which we are seeking assistance and expertise.
“For countries in the search corridors, we are requesting radar and satellite information, as well as specific assets for the search and rescue operation.
“This responsibility is not only to the families and to the investigation, but also the search and rescue operation.
