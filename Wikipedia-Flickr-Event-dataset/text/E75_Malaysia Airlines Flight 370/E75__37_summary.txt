Jan-2014 marked the first time in eight months MAS’ load factor was below 80%.
MAS’ load factor has slipped in the four months since MH370 but only slightly.
All Malaysian carriers typically reduce capacity during Ramadan, particularly domestic capacity as Malaysians elect to stay home during the fasting month.
MAS’ domestic ASKs were down 10% in Jul-2014 while international ASKs were still up by 5% year over year.
