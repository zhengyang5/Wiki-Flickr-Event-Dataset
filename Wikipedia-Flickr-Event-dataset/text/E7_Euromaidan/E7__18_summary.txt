Crimean Tatars protest Crimean parliament's refusal to recognize new authorities26-02-2014 11:54Nearly 5,000 Crimean Tatars and supporters of the Euromaidan-Crimea movement gathered in front of the Crimean Supreme Council in Simferopol on Wednesday to protest the Crimean parliament's refusal to recognize the new Ukrainian authorities.
They held Ukrainian and Crimean-Tatar national flags and chanted "Ukraine!
At the same time, several hundred supporters of pro-Russian organizations and the Russian Unity Party gathered near the parliament building with Russian flags to support the Crimean parliament.
Minor scuffles and clashes were reported while the Crimean parliament was holding an extraordinary plenary session.
The prime item on the agenda was a Crimean referendum on secession from Ukraine.
