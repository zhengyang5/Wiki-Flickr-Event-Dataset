Image caption Rescue officials say they do not know exactly how many people are still missing after the disasterThe death toll from the collapse of an eight-storey factory building near the Bangladeshi capital, Dhaka, has passed 700, officials say.
The death toll from Bangladesh now stands at 705.
Officials say about 2,500 people were injured in the collapse and that 2,437 people have been rescued.
Working conditionsIt came as hundreds of garment workers who survived the collapse protested by blocking a highway close to the accident site demanding unpaid wages and benefits.
The EU has said it is considering "appropriate action" to encourage an improvement in working conditions in Bangladesh factories.
