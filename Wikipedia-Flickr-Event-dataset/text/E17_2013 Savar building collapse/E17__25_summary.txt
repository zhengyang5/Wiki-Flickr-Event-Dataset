Try another or register with your social accountAt least 100 people were killed today and 700 injured when an eight-storey building housing four clothing factories collapsed on the outskirts of the Bangladesh capital Dhaka.
The army, emergency workers and volunteers were searching the debris of Rana Plaza for trapped survivors.
Local police chief Mohammad Asaduzzaman told Reuters news agency that factory owners appeared to have ignored a warning not to allow workers in.
Last November 112 workers were killed in a blaze at the Tazreen factory in a nearby industrial suburb.
