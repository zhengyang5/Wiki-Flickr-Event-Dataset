PyeongChang 2018IOC’s Olympic Athlete from Russia Implementation Group releases guidelines for uniforms, accessories and equipmentFollowing a meeting with representatives of the suspended Russian Olympic Committee last week the OARIG has established a list of principles relating to the design of the OAR’s uniforms (ceremonies, competition, training and casual), accessories and equipment.
