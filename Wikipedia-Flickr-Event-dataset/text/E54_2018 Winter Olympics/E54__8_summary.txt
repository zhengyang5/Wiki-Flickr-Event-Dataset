Press releasesInternational Olympic Committee (IOC) President Jacques Rogge has appointed Ms Gunilla Lindberg to chair the Coordination Commission for the XXIII Olympic Winter Games in PyeongChang, Republic of Korea, in 2018.
PyeongChang was awarded the 2018 Olympic Winter Games last month in Durban.
