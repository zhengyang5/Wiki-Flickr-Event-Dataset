Thousands of South Koreans cheered as Pyeongchang defeated two European cities to become the host of the 2018 Winter Olympics.
The decision will mean billions of dollars in investment for the mountain resort.
Pyeongchang, South Korea, on Wednesday defeated two European cities to win the right to host the 2018 Winter Olympics, bringing the Winter Games to Asia for only the third time in history.
Pyeongchang won in the first round of voting, beating out Munich, Germany, and Annecy, France, a resort town in the French Alps.
