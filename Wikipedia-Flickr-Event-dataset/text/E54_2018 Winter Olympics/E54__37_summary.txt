2018 Olympic Winter Games Men: The 2018 Olympic Winter Games will take place in PyeongChang, Korea, 9-25 February 2018.
The men’s and women’s ice hockey tournaments are scheduled to take place at venues in the city of Gangneung.
Olympic Qualification Preliminary Round 2 (11-14 February 2016)Group G: Italy (18), Great Britain (23), Netherlands (24), Serbia (29).
Note: The qualifiers for the Olympics and the various qualification tournaments will be entered in the order according to their position in the 2015 IIHF Men’s World Ranking.
Note: The qualifiers for the Olympics and the various qualification tournaments will be entered in the order according to their position in the 2016 IIHF Women’s World Ranking.
