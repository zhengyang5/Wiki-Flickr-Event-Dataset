Ubisoft’s open-world snowboarding game Steep will receive its first full expansion this winter.
Steep: Road to the Olympics will launch Dec. 5 and feature content inspired by the 2018 Winter Olympics.
The expansion will arrive just ahead of the Winter Olympics in Pyeongchang, South Korea.
Players will get to actually participate in a virtual version of next year’s Winter Olympic Games as part of the upcoming Road to the Olympics expansion.
Steep: Road to the Olympics will be available on PlayStation 4, Windows PC and Xbox One when it launches in December.
