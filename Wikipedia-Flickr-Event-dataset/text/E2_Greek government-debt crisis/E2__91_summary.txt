Greek government debt stands at 323 billion euros ($366 billion), over 175% of the country's GDP.
EurozoneMost Greek debt is held by other eurozone countries.
Germany's exposure to the bailouts totals 56 billion euros -- equivalent to roughly 700 euros per citizen.
IMFThe IMF holds 32 billion euros of Greek debt.
Even countries that defaulted on their IMF debt in the past, such as Argentina, ended up paying the IMF in full.
