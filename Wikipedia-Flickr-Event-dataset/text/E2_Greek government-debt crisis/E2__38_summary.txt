The measures, representing a break from the past, aim to bring Greece’s public finances under control and modernize the Greek economy.
Subject to reviewsThe eurozone will contribute roughly two-thirds of the total financial assistance and the 186-member IMF one-third.
IMF support will be provided under a three-year €30 billion (about $40 billion) Stand-By Arrangement (SBA)—the IMF’s standard lending instrument.
Greece’s defining moment“This is a defining moment for Greece,” said Poul Thomsen, who heads the IMF negotiating mission to Athens.
Accordingly, the Greek government plans to implement rigorous fiscal measures, far-reaching structural policies, and financial sector reforms.
