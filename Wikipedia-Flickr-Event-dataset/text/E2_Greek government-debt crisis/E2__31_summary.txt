In 2010 an agreement was signed on Swiss participation in EU education, professional training and youth programmes[1].
Following a meeting between President Juncker and former Swiss President Sommaruga in February 2015, informal consultations between the Commission and the Swiss authorities on a possible understanding have been launched.
On 1 March 2016, Switzerland ratified the Agreement on the modalities of the participation of the Swiss Confederation in the work of the European Asylum Support Office.
