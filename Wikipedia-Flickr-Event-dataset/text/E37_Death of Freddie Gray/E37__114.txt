Evening protest of Freddie Gray death planned in Miami area Posted: Saturday, May 2, 2015 1:59 PM EDT Updated: Saturday, May 2, 2015 1:59 PM EDT

MIAMI GARDENS, Fla. (AP) - Activists are expected to gather at Miami Gardens City Hall late Saturday to join in nationwide protests against the death of Baltimore man while in police custody.

The Miami event is just one of dozens of protests in cities against the death of Freddie Gray. Gray died April 12 from spinal cord injuries. Six Baltimore police officers have been charged in his death.

The Miami Gardens event is scheduled to start at 7 p.m. Organizers say they do not how many people might join in the South Florida protest.
