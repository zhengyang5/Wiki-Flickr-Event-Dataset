Violence and looting overtook much of West Baltimore on Monday, injuring more than a dozen police officers and leaving buildings and vehicles in flames.
About 10 p.m., police confirmed shots were fired at an officer in the area of Virginia Avenue and Reisterstown Road in Northwest Baltimore.
(Colin Campbell/Baltimore Sun) Protesters in Baltimore Monday throw bricks, bottles and other objects at police.
Nearby, they looted a CVS drug store, which store officials said had already closed, before it caught fire.
Two city recreation centers in West Baltimore, the Robert C. Marshall Recreation Center in Upton and Lillian Jones Recreation Center in Sandtown-Winchester, closed early.
