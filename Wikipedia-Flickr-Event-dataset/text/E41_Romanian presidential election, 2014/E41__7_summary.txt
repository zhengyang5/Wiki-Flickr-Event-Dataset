The President of Romania ARTICLE 80(1) The President of Romania shall represent the Romanian State and is the safeguard of the national independence, unity and territorial integrity of the country.
(2) The President of Romania shall guard the observance of the Constitution and the proper functioning of the public authorities.
Election of the President ARTICLE 81(1) The President of Romania shall be elected by universal, equal, direct, secret an free suffrage.
Acts of the President ARTICLE 100(1) In the exercise of his powers, the President of Romania shall issue decrees, which shall be published in the Official Gazette of Romania.
(6) The President of Romania shall preside over the proceedings of the Superior Council of Magistracy he takes part in.
