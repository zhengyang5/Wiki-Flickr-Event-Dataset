Dale Earnhardt Jr. will be staying with Hendrick Motorsports for another five Sprint Cup seasons, keeping him with the NASCAR team through the end of 2017.
"We're excited to have everything formalized and announced," team owner Rick Hendrick said in a statement.

Earnhardt Jr. spent his first eight Cup seasons with Dale Earnhardt Inc., the team founded by his late father.
He signed with Hendrick in June 2007 and joined the team in 2008.
