Justin Bieber has visited the Philippines to help victims of Typhoon Haiyan.
The teen hearthrob arrived from Australia and flew to Tacloban, the devastated capital of central Leyte province.
The 19-year-old pop star visited a school being used as an evacuation centre and played basketball with fans.
He also performed an acoustic set to the delight of a crowd of residents.
Bieber has used his worldwide profile to help raise £389,705 so far, with a goal of reaching £611,098 ($1 million) to support Unicef's efforts to help those affected.
