This is OCHA Flash Update #3 on Typhoon Haiyan in relation to the Federated States of Micronesia (FSM) and Palau.
Typhoon Haiyan was upgraded to a Super Typhoon overnight as it passed over the islands of Yap and Ngulu in Yap state, FSM.
As Haiyan tracks west-northwest towards the central Philippines, all watches and warnings have been cancelled for FSM and Palau.
Federated States of MicronesiaInitial damage assessments by the Government and International Organization for Migration (IOM) continue in Yap State.
On Yap Island, the villages of Epibnaw, Weloy, Fanif and Colonia report minor damage, although most fruit-bearing trees have been destroyed.
