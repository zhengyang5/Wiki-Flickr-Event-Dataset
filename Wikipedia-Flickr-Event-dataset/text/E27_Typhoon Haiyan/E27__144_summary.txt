Another relief and medical mission was conducted in Carigara, near Tacloban City on Nov. 16 where some 30,000 more residents were given relief goods.
“The INC Church Administration saw the need for a bigger relief and medical mission for the survivors of the typhoon.
The relief distribution and medical mission started around 10 a.m. lasted until 4 p.m.Thousands of people lined up.
The distribution was done fast, with 15 relief distribution tables set up in the entire plaza.
The INC “Lingap sa Mamamayan” for Yolanda survivors is set to hold a massive relief distribution and medical mission in Tacloban City on Friday, Nov. 29.
