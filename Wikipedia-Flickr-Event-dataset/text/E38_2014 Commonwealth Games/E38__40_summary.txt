In the upcoming Commonwealth Games to be held at Glasgow/Scotland form 23rd July till 3rd August 2014, Maldives will be participating in 5 different sports.
Maldivian contingent for the CWG2014 will consist of a total of 25 athletes form Swimming, Athletics, Table Tennis, Shooting and Badminton.
