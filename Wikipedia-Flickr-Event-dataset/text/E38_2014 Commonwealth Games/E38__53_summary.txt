The Sri Lankan contingent comprises 104 athletes and nearly 45 officials.
The chef-de-mission on tour is Nishanthe Indunil Piyasena who is the Assistant Secretary of the National Olympic Committee of Sri Lanka.
Piyasena was appointed chairman of the Development/Special project of the Cycling Federation of Sri Lanka earlier this year.
He is also the treasurer of the Sri Lanka Triathlon, former vice president and secretary general of the Cycling Federation of Sri Lanka.
