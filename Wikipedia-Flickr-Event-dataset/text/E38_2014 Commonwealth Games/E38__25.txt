THE national contingent left the Sultanate today to compete at the 20th Commonwealth Games in Glasgow, Scotland.

National cyclist Muhammad I’maadi Abd Aziz will be the sole representative for Brunei at the July 23 to August 3 event.

The 22-year-old is set to take part in two track events (Point Race and Scratch) and the road race event (Mass Start).
