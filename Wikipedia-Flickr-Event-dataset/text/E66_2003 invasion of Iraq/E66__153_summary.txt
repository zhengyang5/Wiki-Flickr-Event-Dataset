As we head toward a presidential election, mainstream media and their pundits are telling us Bush will be difficult to beat.
What mass media don't discuss much is their own role in public opinion and public ignorance, two measures that run hand in hand.
Americans who are fundamentally misinformed about 9/11 provide the bulk of those tallied in polls as supporting Bush and the Iraq war.
Limbaugh rose to be the top commentator in our country while conducting a reign of error virtually unnoticed by mainstream media.
