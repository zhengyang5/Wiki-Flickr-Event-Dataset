Marketing Iraq: Why now?
By William SchneiderCNN Senior Political AnalystWASHINGTON (CNN) -- There's a big question hanging over President Bush's Iraq policy: Why now?
Some people are asking, is President Bush's Iraq offensive being driven by the fall election?
Why did the Administration wait until September to make its case against Iraq?
White House chief of staff Andrew Card told The New York Times last week, ``From a marketing point of view, you don't introduce new products in August.''
