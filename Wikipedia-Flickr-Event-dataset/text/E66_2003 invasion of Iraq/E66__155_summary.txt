In most western countries, opinion polls show clearly that a majority would accept a war if it were authorised by the UN.
It is about whether a peaceful solution can still be found, as the marchers hope, or whether war is the only option.
Mr Hussein has maintained his tight grip on power, through brutal force, black-market revenues and his control of permitted oil earnings.
But Mr Hussein still needs to be contained or disarmed.
But, in the end, the reality remains: if Mr Hussein refuses to disarm, it would be right to go to war.
