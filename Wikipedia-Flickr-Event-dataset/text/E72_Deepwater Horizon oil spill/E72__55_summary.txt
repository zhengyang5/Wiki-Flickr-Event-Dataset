Release date: 24 May 2010BP today provided an update on developments in the response to the MC252 oil well incident in the Gulf of Mexico.
Work goes on to optimise the oil and gas collected from the damaged riser through the riser insertion tube tool (RITT).
The US Government has appointed a flow rate technical team (FRTT) to determine the well flow rate.
Surface Spill Response and Containment Work continues to collect and disperse oil that has reached the surface of the sea.
The cost of the response to date amounts to about $760 million, including the cost of the spill response, containment, relief well drilling, grants to the Gulf states, claims paid and federal costs.
