The report, “Restoring a Degraded Gulf of Mexico: Wildlife and Wetlands Three Years into the Gulf Oil Disaster,” found that dolphins were among the hardest hit animals.
* More than 1,700 sea turtles were found stranded between May 2010 and November 2012 — the last date for which information is available.
For comparison, on average about 240 sea turtles are stranded annually.
* Scientists found that the oil disaster affected the cellular function of the killifish, a common baitfish at the base of the food web.
