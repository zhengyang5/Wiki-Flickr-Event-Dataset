An oil sheen discovered last month in the Gulf of Mexico near the site of the Deepwater Horizon rig explosion matches samples from the 2010 BP Plc (BP/) oil spill.
BP reported the sheen on Sept. 16 about 50 miles (80 kilometers) off the coast of Louisiana and the U.S. took samples 10 days later, the Coast Guard said in a statement on its website yesterday.
The size of the sheen has varied depending on conditions, the Coast Guard said.
