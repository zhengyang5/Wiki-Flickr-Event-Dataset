ReleaseAdler hosted today the celebrations dedicated to the startup of the Adler thermal power plant (TPP) construction.
Construction of the Adler TPP is another proof of the fact that Gazprom is nowadays the most effective and reliable investor in the Russian power generation sector.
Novel engineering and architectural solutions will be applied during the Adler TPP construction, so that the station could blend into the unique natural landscape.
Mosenergo fuel and energy company is the general contractor for the Adler TPP construction.
The Adler TPP is to be completed in 2012 according to the Olympic facilities construction schedule.
