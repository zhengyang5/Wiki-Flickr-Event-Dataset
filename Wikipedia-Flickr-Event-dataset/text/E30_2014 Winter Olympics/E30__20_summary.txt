(Photo: Igor Yakunin, AP)Sochi celebrated 500 days until the Opening Ceremony of the 2014 Winter Olympics with a national sports festival, various flash mobs and the release of the official slogan for the upcoming Games, "Hot.

("Keep the hot side hot, and the cool side cool," was the actual one.)
