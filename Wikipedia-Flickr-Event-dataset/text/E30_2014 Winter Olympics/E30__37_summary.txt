This is an Olympic city, and here’s the jeans-clad mayor striding into his office on a recent afternoon, fresh from a landslide, and not the electoral kind.
President Vladimir Putin signed a law at the end of June prohibiting the promotion of nontraditional sexual relationships to minors.
It has been interpreted as banning gay pride parades — children might see them — and preventing any discussion of homosexuality among teenagers.
The law has made gay athletes and spectators fearful of discrimination, and even arrest, at the Olympics.
