The polls in Wales suggest a very strong Labour performance there, with the party on the edge of an overall majority.
Scottish polling is less regular, but they too suggest a good result for Labour.
However, ask people if they trust Labour or the Conservatives on the economy and the Tories still come out top.
There will be pressure upon Miliband to simply oppose all the cuts and reap the rewards of public unhappiness.
The short term position for Labour is good, and will get even better next year.
