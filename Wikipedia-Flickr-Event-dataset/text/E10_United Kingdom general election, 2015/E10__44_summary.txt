The lack of black and ethnic minority candidates in the Green party is a problem that needs fixing, Natalie Bennett has admitted.
Only 4 per cent of Green candidates are from minority communities, according to a study by University College London – the lowest proportion of all the other major party, including Ukip.
“I would agree that our percentage of BME candidates is disappointing and it’s something we very much want to focus on,” she said.
Even Nigel Farage’s Ukip fares better, with 6 per cent of its candidates coming from a BME background.
