The BBC election team has been collating information on all the men and women standing around the United Kingdom.
This election sees the highest percentage of female candidates ever with just over a quarter - 26.1%.
The Green Party, lead by Natalie Bennett, has the highest percentage of female candidates - 37.5%.
From the other parties; 26.1% of Conservative candidates are women, 26.3% of Liberal Democrat candidates are women and 25% of Plaid Cymru candidates are women.
It is lowest in Northern Ireland, where 23.9% of candidates are women,Within the English regions, London has the highest ratio with 30.4% of candidates being women.
