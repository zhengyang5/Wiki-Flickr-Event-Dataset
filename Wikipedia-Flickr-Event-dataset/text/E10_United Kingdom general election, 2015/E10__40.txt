Politicians had until 4pm on Thursday to submit their nomination papers, with the full list then released a short time later.

It can now be viewed on the Electoral Office website.

Candidates are standing for election across Northern Ireland’s 18 constituencies.

Last time there were seats held by eight DUP MPs, five from Sinn Féin, three from the SDLP, one Alliance and one independent.

People in Northern Ireland can register to vote by printing off a form from the website of the Electoral Office, which can also be used to apply for an Electoral Identity Card.

The deadline for registering to vote at the UK Parliamentary election is 20 April, while the deadline for applying for an Electoral ID Card is 24 April.

Completed forms should be returned to your Area Electoral Office, details of which are also available from the Electoral Office website.

The General Election will be held on 7 May.
