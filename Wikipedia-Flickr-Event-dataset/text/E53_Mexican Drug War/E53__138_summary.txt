ReutersDrug trafficking organizations in Mexico are using drones to transport drugs, said the United States Drug Enforcement Agency.
Drones have been called the ideal 'drug mules' for the speed and efficiency with which they can transport drugs.
According to Defense One, the President requested $39 million to create a surveillance program, including the use of unmanned aircraft.
