MEXICO UNDER SIEGE Anti-drug general oustedSergio Aponte Polito is adored by the public for fighting traffickers and corrupt officials.
Aponte policed a region that serves as a major drug-trafficking corridor for some of Mexico's most powerful criminal groups, including Tijuana's notorious Arellano Felix cartel.
The more than 3,000 troops under his command arrested 1,388 suspects and seized 539 tons of marijuana, 4 tons of cocaine and 1,583 weapons.
U.S. agencies viewed the general as a strong ally in the drug war.
He also said a former deputy attorney general had been protecting organized crime groups.
