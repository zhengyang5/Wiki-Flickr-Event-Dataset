DISCUSSION AND 48-HOUR OUTLOOK ------------------------------ At 1100 PM AST (0300 UTC), the center of Post-Tropical Cyclone Joaquin was located near latitude 42.0 North, longitude 37.0 West.
The post-tropical cyclone is moving toward the east near 35 mph (56 km/h), and this general motion with a decrease in forward speed is expected over the next couple of days.
HAZARDS AFFECTING LAND ---------------------- WIND: Gale-force winds associated with the post-tropical cyclone are expected to spread over portions of the Azores on Thursday.
