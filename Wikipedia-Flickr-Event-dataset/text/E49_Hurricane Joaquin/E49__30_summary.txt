By NICO SCAVELLATribune Staff Reporternscavella@tribunemedia.netALL cruise ships due to arrive in New Providence on Friday have been cancelled due to Hurricane Joaquin, Ministry of Tourism officials have announced.
Joy Jibrilu, Director General of Tourism, said three cruise ships expected to arrive today have been "diverted" to various locations.
She said that with the exception of one cruise line, all cruise ships will return to the New Providence cruise port, while those scheduled to arrive in Grand Bahama will do so "as scheduled".
"All cruise ships that were due to arrive at the port of Nassau today have been cancelled," Mrs Jibrilu said.
