St. Louis County Prosecutor Bob McCulloch gave the following definition for strong-arm robbery:"The use of physical force in a robbery.
If someone is stealing, and another person makes an attempt to stop them, and then physical force is used to complete the act that is strong-arm robbery.
If a weapon is used in a robbery, then the charge would be 'robbery in the 1st degree.'"
