(Reuters)The Michael Brown grand jury has spoken, declining to return charges against Officer Darren Wilson.
Contrary to the complaints of some critics, the grand jury process was clearly fair.
A day before the grand jury’s decision was announced, Michael Brown’s family attorney raised the objection that the grand jury process was unfair because it was deviation from the normal process.
The difference in the outcome with the Michael Brown investigative grand jury from a routine federal prosecution is hardly surprising.
