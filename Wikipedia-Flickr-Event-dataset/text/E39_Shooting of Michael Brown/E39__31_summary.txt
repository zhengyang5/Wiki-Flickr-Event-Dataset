Louis Post-Dispatch via AP)The FBI has opened an investigation into the shooting of Michael Brown, the 18-year-old killed in suburban St. Louis over the weekend.
Brown, an unarmed black teenager, was shot and killed in Ferguson, a small city outside St. Louis, on Saturday afternoon.
“We have initiated a civil rights investigation,” Cheryl Mimura, spokeswoman for the FBI in St. Louis, told The Washington Post on Monday morning.
The FBI will continue to monitor the investigation that is being carried out by the St. Louis County Police Department.
That department was asked by the police in Ferguson to handle the investigation into the shooting.
