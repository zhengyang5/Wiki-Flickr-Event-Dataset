Black lives matter?
And for the rest of us to view black life as worthy of defending, to unequivocally repeat that black lives matter in a world that insists otherwise, is a radical act – if a tiresome one.
Community members on the ground in Ferguson and in the surrounding areas have already been demonstrating the power of transformative, compassionate organizing.
We may not have control of a justice system that suppresses the people it’s supposed to protect, but we have each other.
And even when the grandest of grand juries dares to deny the sanctity of our lives while the world watches, we still matter.
