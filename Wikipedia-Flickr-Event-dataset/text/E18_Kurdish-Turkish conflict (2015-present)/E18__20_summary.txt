MILITARY CONVOYThe latest security operations, which media reports said were being conducted with 10,000 police and troops backed by tanks, were the largest since the end of the ceasefire.
Hundreds of people in Istanbul as well as Diyarbakir and eastern city of Van took to the streets to protest against the security operations and curfews.
One Turkish soldier hurt in clashes in Cizre on Saturday has died of his wounds, security sources said.
