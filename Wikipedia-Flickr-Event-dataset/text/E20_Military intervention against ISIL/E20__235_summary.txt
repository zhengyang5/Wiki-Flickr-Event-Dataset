DoD News, Defense Media ActivityStraps secure water bundles aboard a C-17 Globemaster III before a humanitarian airdrop over Iraq, Aug. 8, 2014.
The 816th Expeditionary Airlift Squadron aircrew airdropped 40 bundles of water for displaced citizens near Sinjar, Iraq.
U.S. Air Force photoTonight, the U.S. military conducted another successful airdrop of food and water for thousands of Iraqi citizens threatened by the Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant on Mount Sinjar, Iraq, Defense Department officials said.
The C-17 dropped 40 container delivery system bundles of packaged meals, complemented by a C-130 loaded with an additional 16 bundles totaling 28,224 meals.
In addition, one C-130 dropped 16 bundles totaling 1,522 gallons of fresh drinking water.
