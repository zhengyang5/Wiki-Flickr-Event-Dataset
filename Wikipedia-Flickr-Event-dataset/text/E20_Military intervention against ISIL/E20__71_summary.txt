Key al-Qaeda figure Muhsin al-Fadhli killed in U.S. airstrike in Syria - PentagonPUBLISHED Tue, July 21, 2015 - 4:19pm EDT Photo: Rewards For JusticeKey al-Qaeda figure Muhsin al-Fadhli, the leader of the so-called Khorasan Group which is made up of senior al-Qaeda members, has been killed in a U.S. airstrike in northwestern Syria, according to Pentagon spokesman Captain Jeff Davis.
"His death will degrade and disrupt ongoing external operations of al-Qaeda against the United States and our allies and partners," Davis said.
Jeff Davis (click to expand) The following statement may be attributed to Capt.
Jeff Davis, Director of Press Operations:"Muhsin al-Fadhli, a longtime al-Qaeda operative, was killed in a kinetic strike July 8 while traveling in a vehicle near Sarmada, Syria.
His death will degrade and disrupt ongoing external operations of al-Qaeda against the United States and our allies and partners."
