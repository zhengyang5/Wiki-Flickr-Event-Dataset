Department of StateEU High Representative Federica Mogherini attended the first ministerial level meeting of the Global Coalition to Counter the Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant (ISIL), which was held at the invitation of U.S. Secretary of State John Kerry in Brussels on 3 December 2014.
The meeting brought together ministers from sixty partner countries to discuss the way forward in the fight against ISIL/Daesh.
The EU High Representative's presence at the Global coalition meeting underlines the importance the EU attaches to the fight against ISIL/Daesh.
Speaking in Brussels HR/VP Mogherini said: "Islam cannot and should not be identified with ISIL, which is a terrorist organisation and has nothing to do with religion".
