To date 70 people have been formally identified, having died following the fire at Grenfell Tower.
Also, baby Logan Gomes who was stillborn in hospital on 14 June, has been recorded by police as a victim of the fire.
The following victims have been formally identified in agreement with the Coroner, Dr Fiona Wilcox.
- A 71-year-old woman, Victoria King- A 40-year-old woman, Alexandra AtalaA total of 32 other people have been formally identified but have not been named at the request of their families.
The families of all 71 victims, including baby Logan, have been informed, and are being supported by specially trained family liaison officers.
