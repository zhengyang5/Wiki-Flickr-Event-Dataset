Proceeds from the Community Shield match at Wembley will be donated to those affected by the Grenfell Tower fire.

The FA chairman, Greg Clarke, said: “Like the rest of the country, we were all deeply saddened by the terrible incident at Grenfell Tower.
“Whilst only a football match, we hope that in some small way through the Community Shield we can help.
We play the FA Community Shield on Sunday, August 6, with the victims and the heroes of this terrible tragedy close to our hearts.”The English Football League said its clubs would give up their share of Community Shield proceeds, with the EFL topping up that sum to make a £175,000 total donation.
