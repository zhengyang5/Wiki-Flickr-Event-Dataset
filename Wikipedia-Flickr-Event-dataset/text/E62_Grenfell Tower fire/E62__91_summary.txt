Hundreds of people died in the Grenfell Tower tragedy, Diane Abbott has claimed.
The current death toll is at 79 but the shadow Home Secretary said she expected this to rise into the hundreds.
“Grenfell Tower is not just an accident; Grenfell Tower is not just an unfortunate incident.
Those hundreds of people that died are a direct consequence of Tory attitudes in social housing,” Ms Abbott told a conference of the Labour Progress group.
