Image copyright PA Image caption Emeli Sande joins other stars to record charity singleStars including Emeli Sande, Pixie Lott, and Stormzy have gathered to record a Grenfell Tower charity single.
The cover of Simon and Garfunkel's hit Bridge Over Troubled Water was organised by Simon Cowell.
Other stars including Rita Ora and Leona Lewis recorded parts for the song on Saturday.
