A massive wildfire nicknamed "the beast" has forced a work camp north of Fort McMurray housing first responders to evacuate Saturday night.
Non-essential staff are currently being sent south of Fort McMurray to safe sites on buses.
Some workers will be sent to Grey Wolf, another camp north of Fort McMurray.
"If you aren't a police officer, a firefighter or otherwise have a first-responder role in the emergency, you should not be in Fort McMurray."
