The Prime Minister of the Czech Republic Jiri Rusnok has sent a letter of condolence to the President of the Republic of South Africa Jacob Zuma in connection with the death of the former President of the Republic of South Africa and the Nobel Peace Prize Laureat Nelson Mandela.
Your Excellency,I was deeply saddened by the news about the decease of former president of the Republic of South Africa and Nobel Prize laureate Mr Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela.
Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela was not only a prominent figure of political and social life in the Republic of South Africa, but also one of the most inspiring world leaders of our times.
I strongly believe that the legacy of Nelson Mandela will live on and become an inspiration for next generations.
Respectfully yours,Jiří RusnokPrime Minister of the Czech Republic
