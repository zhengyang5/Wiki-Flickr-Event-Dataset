Islanders Pay Tribute To Nelson MandelaTributes have been paid to Nelson Mandela locally.
Guernsey's longest serving politician, Deputy Mary Lowe, has seen for herself the cell on Robben Island where he was held in solitary confinement for 19 years.
The Mother of the House says it proved to her what a remarkable man Mr Mandela was.
Guernsey's Chief Minister agreed Nelson Mandela was a truly great man.
Deputy Peter Harwood says he served as an inspiration to all generations across all nations, and was an exemplar of the true meaning of reconciliation.
