Indian Political Leaders have mourned the death of South African Anti Apartheid Hero and its first President Bharat Ratna Nelson Mandela .
President Pranab Mukherjee said in his condolence message, that Mandela was an “icon of inspiration for humanity”.
“His life and work will remain a source of eternal inspiration for generations to come.
Commerce Minister Anand Sharma said, “Mandela had enormous tenacity of purpose, courage of conviction and profound humility.
He truly was a hero and a source of inspiration for generations of young people, activists and freedom fighters.
