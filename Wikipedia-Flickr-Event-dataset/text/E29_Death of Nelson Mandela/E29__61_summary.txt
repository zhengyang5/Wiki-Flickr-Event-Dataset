Mr Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela, Former President of the Republic of South Africa[Speeches and Statements]Upon receiving the news of the passing of H.E.
Mr Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela, former President of the Republic of South Africa, I was filled with great sadness.
Former President Mandela was a man of conviction.
Not only was former President Mandela a tireless fighter, he was also a promoter of reconciliation.
On behalf of my Government and the people of Japan, I offer my sincerest condolences to the people of South Africa upon the passing of former President Mandela.
