EFF will carry on Madiba’s battle – Julius MalemaThe Economic Freedom Fighters will take up former president Nelson Mandela’s struggle for freedom, says EFF leader Julius Malema.
He led a crowd of EFF members to Mandela’s house in Houghton today to lay flowers and pay tribute to him a week after his death.
