Wolinski co-founded the satirical magazine L'Enragé with Siné during the student revolts in 1968.
He was one of the founders of Charlie Hebdo.
Jean CabutCabut, who worked under the name "Cabu," was the lead cartoonist for Charlie Hebdo.
In a February 2006 issue of Charlie Hebdo, a Cabu cartoon depicted the Muslim prophet Muhammad, under the caption "Muhammad overwhelmed by fundamentalists,” crying : " So hard to be loved by jerks!"
Cabu, who appeared regularly on French TV, was the father of the French singer and songwriter Mano Solo, who died in 2010.
