(Mohammad Sajjad/Associated Press)PESHAWAR, Pakistan — A hard-line cleric in northwest Pakistan has led a memorial service for the two brothers who attacked the satirical newspaper Charlie Hebdo in Paris, praising their assault.
Pir Mohammad Chishti, who runs a religious seminary in the city of Peshawar, led the prayers Tuesday.
Charlie Hebdo often lampooned organized religions, including Islam by drawing cartoons of the Prophet Muhammad.
