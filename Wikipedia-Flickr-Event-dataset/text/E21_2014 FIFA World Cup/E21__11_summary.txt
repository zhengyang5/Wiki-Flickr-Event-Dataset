Rui Rodrigues presented the necessary documents to FIFA President Joseph S. Blatter on behalf of the Confederacao Brasileira de Futebol.
Brazil is now the only remaining candidate for the right to host the 2014 FIFA World Cup™ after the Colombian football association officially withdrew its candidature on 11 April.
In a letter addressed to FIFA, Colombian FA president Luis H. Bedoya Giraldo stated that the Colombian FA had decided to withdraw from the bidding procedure.
Brazil now has until the end of July 2007 to complete its bid, which must contain all of the necessary government guarantees.
The FIFA Executive Committee is set to name the host nation of the 2014 FIFA World Cup™ in November 2007.
