A quick look at some interesting statistics to come out of the Round of 16 at the FIFA World Cup:The numbers8 – Number of group winners to make it to the quarter-finals.
This is the first time all eight group winners have advanced to the quarter-finals.
2.25 – Goals-per-game average in the Round of 16, a drop from the 2.83 average in the group phase154 – Total goals to this point in the World Cup, already surpassing the 2010 tournament.
5 – Matches that went to extra time in the Round of 16, the most since the stage was implemented in 1986.
7 – Goals in extra time in those five matches, compared to 11 scored in normal time overall in the Round of 16.
