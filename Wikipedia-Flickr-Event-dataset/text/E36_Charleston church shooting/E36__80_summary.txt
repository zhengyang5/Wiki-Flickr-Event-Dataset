CLOSE Dylann Roof is charged with nine counts of murder, three new counts of attempted murder, and one count of possessing a weapon during the commission of a violent crime.
Dylann Roof, 21, is accused in the June 17 massacre that left nine people dead at Charleston's predominantly black Emanuel AME Church.
Roof was arrested in Shelby, N.C., 245 miles from Charleston, about 14 hours after the attack.
Charleston Police Chief Gregory Mullen said Roof was cooperative when Shelby police took took him into custody.
Roof has already been charged by state warrants with the murder counts and possessing a weapon during commission of violent crime.
