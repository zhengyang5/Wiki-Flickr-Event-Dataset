After photos of suspected killer Dylann Roof were released this week, his father and uncle contacted police and positively identified Roof and his car, according to newly released Charleston police warrants for his arrest.
Add InterestHis father also told investigators Roof, 21, owned a .45-caliber handgun, the warrants say.
"We are getting cooperation at this point," the law enforcement official told ABC News today.
Roof spent Thursday night in protective custody and on suicide watch at the Sheriff Al Cannon Detention Center in North Charleston, according to the Charleston County Sheriff's Office.
He has been assigned public defender Ashley Pennington, according to court personnel.
