Dylann Roof's family released a new statement Thursday as the first funerals were held for Ethel Lance and other victims of the June 18 shooting at Emanuel African Methodist Episcopal Church in Charleston, South Carolina.
Roof, 21, faces nine counts of murder and one weapon charge after he allegedly shot and killed nine people during a Bible study at the historic black church.
Speaking through attorney Boyd Young, Roof's family once again expressed condolences to the victims' families and said they would postpone further comment out of respect to mourners.
