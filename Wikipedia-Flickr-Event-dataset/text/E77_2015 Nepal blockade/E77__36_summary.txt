Yesterday, Prime Minister Koirala asked India to resume the cross-border transportation without any delay.
Addressing the Parliament, he asked India to end the blockade on Nepal without any delay so that Nepal can enjoy its right to transit.
Nepali Congress (NC) senior leader and former Prime Minister Sher Bahadur Deuba also said that India should lift the ongoing obstruction on the border soon.
He pointed out the need to lift the ‘unannounced blockade’ to ease supply of essentials and petroleum products to Nepal.
