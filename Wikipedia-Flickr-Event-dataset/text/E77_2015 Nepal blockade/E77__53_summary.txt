The Nepalgunj office of Maiti Nepal says most of the girls were under 18Jan 1, 2016-More than 400 who entered India from Nepalgunj border point in search of job in the year 2015 have been reported missing, according to Maiti Nepal, an anti-human trafficking organisation.
.A report of Maiti Nepal, Nepalgunj, showed that 461 girls, most of them were under the age of 18, were reported missing by their families.
Only 51 were rescued while the whereabouts of other missing girls remain unknown.
Keshav Koirala, the regional coordinator Maiti Nepal, said they are trying to locate the missing girls with the help of Indian authorities.
The other missing girls are said to be from the districts like Surkhet, Bardiya, Salyan and Rukum.
