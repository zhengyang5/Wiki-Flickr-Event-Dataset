Organising a press meet at Indreni Foodland in New Baneswor today, Bhattarai also resigned as a Legislature Parliament member.
“I have apprised party Chairman Pushpa Kamal Dahal about my decision to quit the party,” he said.
He was elected as a Constituent Assembly, now Legislature Parliament, member from Gorkha constituency 1.
Despite resigning from the party and the parliament, Bhattarai said he will continue serving the country and its people.
He also had differences with party Chairman Dahal and repeatedly remarked for the need of a new political force.
