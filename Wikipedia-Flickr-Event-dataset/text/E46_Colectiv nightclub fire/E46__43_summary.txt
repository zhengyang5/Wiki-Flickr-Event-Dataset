Image copyright Reuters Image caption Romania is holding three days of mourning for the tragedyTwo more people have died of injuries sustained in Friday's Bucharest nightclub fire, doctors say, raising the death toll in the disaster to 29.
The news came as thousands marched silently through the Romanian capital in memory of the victims.
People carried flowers and lit candles at a memorial outside the nightclub.
