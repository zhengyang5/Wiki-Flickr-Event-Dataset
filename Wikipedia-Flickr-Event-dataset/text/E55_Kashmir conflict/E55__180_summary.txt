The Kashmir valley, which includes all of the major towns and villages along the Jhelum river to the north of Srinagar to Handwara and south to Anantnag, has been at the center of the insurgency since 1989.
Most of the major militant groups have political representation through the All-Parties Hurriyat Conference, based in Srinagar.
Many of the groups continue to command popular support in Srinagar and throughout the valley.
