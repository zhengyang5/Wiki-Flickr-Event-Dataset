Mr Zardari has provoked outrage after being reported as saying that Islamic militants fighting Indian rule in Kashmir were "terrorists".
Leading Kashmir separatists have also denounced Mr Zardari.
Mr Zardari made his controversial reference to them as "terrorists" in an interview with the Wall Street Journal.
Prominent politicians fighting for an end to Indian rule in Kashmir joined in the condemnation of Mr Zardari.
'Just cause' In Pakistan itself, Information Minister Sherry Rehman of Mr Zardari's Pakistan People's Party (PPP) sought to clarify Mr Zardari's comments.
