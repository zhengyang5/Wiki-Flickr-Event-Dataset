61% polling was observed in Jharkhand in the third phase of five-phase elections on Tuesday.
A 50,16,657-strong electorate, including 23,55,728 women, were eligible to exercise their franchise to choose among 289 candidates, including 26 women.
Latest Updates:The third phase of Jharkhand polls ended with 61% of the eligible electorate voting.
While Koderma recorded the maximum 16.90 per cent voting in the first two hours, Barkagaon constituency witnessed the minimum at 7.60%, polling officials said.
Eight MLAs are seeking re-elections in this phase, having 103 independent candidates out of a total of 289, including 26 women.
