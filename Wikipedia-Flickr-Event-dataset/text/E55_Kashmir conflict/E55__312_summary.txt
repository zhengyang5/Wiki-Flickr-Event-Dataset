more-inArticle 370 of the Constitution is the current bedrock of the constitutional relationship between Jammu and Kashmir and the rest of India.
One of the features of Article 370 is that a Constitution amendment becomes applicable to J&K only after the President issues an order.
In its implications for Article 370, the High Court verdict has not broken any new ground.
If anything, it is a reiteration of earlier Supreme Court rulings that Article 370 continues to be operative.
Some may find the observation that Article 370 is beyond repeal or abrogation debatable.
