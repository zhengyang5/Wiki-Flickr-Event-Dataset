The European Parliament, on the behalf of the European Union, welcomes the smooth conduct of the first two phases of assembly elections in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir.
The spectacle of youth and women coming out in large numbers to vote bodes well for the functioning of Indian democracy.
The high voter turnout reveals that the people of Kashmir have complete faith in India's democratic system and want to be an integral part of the country.
The European Union would like to congratulate and its democratic system for the conduct of fair elections, unmarred by violence, in Jammu and Kashmir.
The European Union also commits to offer its complete cooperation with the Government of in satisfying the aspirations of the people and establishing peace in Jammu and Kashmir.
