Front Page | Previous Terrorism to feature in talks with Kofi Annan By Our Special Correspondent NEW DELHI, MARCH 9.
Mr. Annan will visit India for three days from March 15 as part of his South Asia tour.
According to a spokesman of the Ministry of External Affairs, India is expected to raise the subject of international terrorism with Mr. Annan.
During his stay, Mr. Annan will meet the Prime Minister, Mr. Atal Behari Vajpayee, and the External Affairs Minister, Mr. Jaswant Singh.
The dispute had caused 80,000 deaths and it was the duty of Mr. Annan to resolve the dispute.
