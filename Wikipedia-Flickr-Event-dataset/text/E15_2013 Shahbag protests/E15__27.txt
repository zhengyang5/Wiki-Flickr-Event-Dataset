Song written, put to tune, recorded and mixed on 12 Feb 2013 at 11.45 PM.

The song salutes the three minutes’ silence observed at Shahbag and elsewhere in Bangladesh on 12 Feb 2013. The song salutes the Great Movement that’s going on in Bangladesh. I, a 64 year old songwriter- musician-singer, am learning from the Young People of Bangladesh.
