At least 40 people have been killed as deadly clashes swept the country after the verdict was announced.
The party was opposed to Bangladeshi independence but denies any role in war crimes committed by pro-Pakistan militias.
War crimes tribunalRecent weeks have seen a series of large and angry protests by anti-Jamaat protesters demanding the execution of Islamist leaders sentenced by the tribunal.
In January, former party leader Abul Kalam Azad was found guilty in absentia of eight charges of crimes against humanity and sentenced to death.
