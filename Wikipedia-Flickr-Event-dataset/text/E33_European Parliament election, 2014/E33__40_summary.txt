Name, registered office, purpose, and durationArticle 1 The organisation, called European Free Alliance, is a Federation of political parties on the European level.
The name European Free Alliance should always be preceded or followed by 'non-profit organisation' or the abbreviation.
MembershipArticle 5 The association shall be composed of full members, observer members, individual members and honorary members.
The General Assembly of the European Free Alliance asbl/vzw establishes the political programme of the party.
In the general assembly all members shall have an equal voting right: each full member shall have one vote.
