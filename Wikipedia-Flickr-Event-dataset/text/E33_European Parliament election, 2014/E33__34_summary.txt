Statement by Sir Graham Watson, President of the ALDE Party:"Olli Rehn and Guy Verhofstadt, the two candidates in the race for selection by the European Liberal Democrats to lead the forthcoming European election campaign, have today reached an agreement."
"The agreement proposes that the two candidates jointly lead the campaign, on an equal footing.
It proposes further that Verhofstadt be the ALDE Party's candidate for Commission President and that Rehn be the party's candidate for one of the other senior posts in the EU, in particular in the field of economic affairs and foreign policy.
Note to editorsAny candidate requires a formal endorsement from ALDE Party member parties to campaign on behalf of the ALDE Party.
