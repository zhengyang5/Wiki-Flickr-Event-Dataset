Commission president debate to be televised EBU debate will be aired by national broadcasters across Europe.
The debate will come one week before the European Parliament elections, which are to be held on 22-25 May.
European political parties are this year nominating candidates for the position of European Commission president in a bid to increase interest in the European Parliament elections.
While it is the member state governments who appoint the Commission president, their choice must be confirmed by the European Parliament.
The Green group have defiantly chosen to nominate two candidates for the single position of European Commission president.
