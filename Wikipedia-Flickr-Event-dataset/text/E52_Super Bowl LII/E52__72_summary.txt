– Dial Global to Continue as the NFL’s Exclusive Network Radio Partner –New York, NY — September 19, 2012 — The National Football League (NFL) and Dial Global, Inc. (NASDAQ: DIAL) today announced a new multi-year agreement for Dial Global to continue as the exclusive network radio partner of the NFL.
Dial Global has been an NFL network radio partner for more than four decades and the exclusive network radio partner since 1987.
Dial Global Sports also delivers specialty programs, including: The NFL Today, NFL Preview, NFL Insider, and NFL Sunday.
All stations airing Dial Global Sports’ NFL Football coverage can be found using the Station Finder located at dialglobalsports.com.
Dial Global has been the NFL network radio rights holder since 1987.
