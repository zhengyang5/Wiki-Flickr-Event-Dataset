Telemundo Deportes wraps up its coverage of the 2017-18 NFL season presenting the exclusive Spanish-language telecast of SUPER BOWL LII, live from U.S. Bank Stadium in Minneapolis, MN.
Telemundo Deportes' coverage of the event will be led by Miguel Gurwitz, the main host of the pre-halftime-and-post game shows.
Additionally, Telemundo Deportes NFL coverage mainstay, Veronica Contreras, will report from the field and on the sideline.
The game will also be streamed live via the Telemundo Deportes En Vivo app and TelemundoDeportes.com.
In addition, Regional Mexican singer and actor El Dasa, star of Universo's original scripted series "El Vato" is confirmed to attend the Super Bowl on behalf of Universo and Telemundo.
