The Super Bowl briefly went dark tonight after what NBC has attributed to a "brief equipment failure" during a commercial break in the first half.
NBC claims that no commercial time nor game time was missed.
From NBC Sports spokesperson:"We had a brief equipment failure that we quickly resolved.
Seizing the opportunity, Tide continued its theme of being in every part of the Super Bowl tonight by tweeting that the nearly 30 second blackout could be a Tide ad.
The outage appeared widespread as many on Twitter used the hashtag #blackout to talk about the black screen they were seeing.
