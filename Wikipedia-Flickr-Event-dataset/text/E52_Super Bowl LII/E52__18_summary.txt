Next four Super Bowl locations Date Host Stadium Feb. 2015 Arizona University of Phoenix Stadium Feb. 2016 San Francisco Levi's Stadium Feb. 2017 Houston Reliant Stadium Feb. 2018 Minnesota New Minnesota StadiumKeeping with the tradition of rewarding teams with new stadiums, Commissioner Roger Goodell announced Tuesday that Minnesota was awarded the right to host Super Bowl LII.
The 52nd Super Bowl is the second for the city of Minneapolis, which hosted Super Bowl XXVI at the recently demolished Metrodome in 1992.
There is a precedent for NFL owners returning to a cold-weather city -- Detroit hosted two Super Bowls, most recently after opening Ford Field for Super Bowl XL.
The city also plans to pour money into downtown upgrades in advance of the Super Bowl.
The new field is set to open in 2016, giving the city plenty of time to work out any kinks in advance of the 2018 Super Bowl.
