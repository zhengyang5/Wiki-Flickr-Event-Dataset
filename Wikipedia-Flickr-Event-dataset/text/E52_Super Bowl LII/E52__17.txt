New Orleans is tied with Miami for most Super Bowls held with 10 apiece. Most of this goes back to the early days before northern cities built domed stadiums and the Super Bowl required a temperate climate. We were postively spoiled back then, hosting the big event in 1970, '72, '75, '78 and '81 before things slowed down. Here's a look at some images from past New Orleans Super Bowls.
