Cynthia Duque, NB Nuevo Leon, was the winner of Nuestra Belleza Mexico (Miss Mexico) 2012, and will represent her country in Miss Universe 2013.
She is 1.76 m-tall.
The pageant took place on Seprtember 1 in Chiapas.
The 1st runner-up, who will compete in Miss International 2012, was Lucero Montemayor, also from Nuevo Leon.
Karen Padilla, from Distrito Federal, finished in 3rd place.
