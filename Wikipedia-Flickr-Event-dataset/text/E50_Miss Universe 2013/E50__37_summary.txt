Amanda crowned Miss Lanka View(s):Twenty-three-year-old Amanda Rathnayake was crowned Miss Sri Lanka for Miss Universe 2013 last night at a glittering event at the Mount Lavinia Hotel.
She will represent Sri Lanka at the Miss.
Simerene Rabot was adjudged the runner up and Kristina Palliyaguruge the second runner-up.
Wijeya Newspapers Ltd was the print media sponsors of the event.
