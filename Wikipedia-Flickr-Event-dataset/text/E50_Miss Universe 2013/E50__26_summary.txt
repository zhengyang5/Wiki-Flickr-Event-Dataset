Nastassja Bolivar has been crowned Miss Nicaragua 2013 title at the 31st edition of Miss Nicaragua beauty contest.
Farah Eslaquit (Miss Nicaragua 2012 winner) crowned Nastassja Bolivar.
Miss Nicaragua 2013 beauty contest took place at the Ruben Dario National Theater on Saturday night of 2nd March, 2013.
Nastassja Bolivar has been selected as Miss Universe Nicaragua 2013 and an official representative of Nicaragua at the 62nd edition of Miss Universe beauty contest where Olivia Culpo will crown her successor.
