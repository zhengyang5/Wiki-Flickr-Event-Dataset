Updated | With a game scheduled to start at 7:05 pm, the Baltimore Orioles canceled their game on Monday at around 6:15 as violent riots rocked Baltimore.
"After consultation with Baltimore City Police Department, tonight’s game between the Orioles & White Sox at Oriole Park has been postponed," the team said in a statement.
"There's a lot of police in the area," a ticketing agent with the stadium told Newsweek by phone.
