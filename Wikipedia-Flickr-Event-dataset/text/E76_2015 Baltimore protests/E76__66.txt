Demonstrations spread across the country Wednesday night to cities including New York, Washington and Boston in response to the death of Freddie Gray, a black man who suffered fatal injuries while in the custody of Baltimore police. Photo: Reuters

BALTIMORE—Protests resumed here Wednesday evening, with hundreds of people marching to City Hall ahead of the city’s 10 p.m. curfew, put in place after violence flared Monday following the funeral of a black man who died in police custody earlier this month.
