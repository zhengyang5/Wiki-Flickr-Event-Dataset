The proposal was one of several efforts Warnock said would be included in an urban improvement agenda that he said would help address the problems that helped produce the April riots.
He said the GBC has pushed for more socially oriented efforts, such as drug treatment, in the past, but its development ideas often receive more attention.
It has been some time since the GBC put forward a legislative agenda that would emphasize social issues, he said.
"Those are issues that we do pay attention to, but I think now it is particularly important," he said.
A call for donations by text to help rebuilding efforts raised more than $200,000, including a $50,000 match by the GBC.
