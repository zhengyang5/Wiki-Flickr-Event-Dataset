World Series ratings continue to impress.
ADVERTISEMENTThe Dodgers’ win delivered the second-largest Saturday World Series audience since 2004 (BOS-STL Game 1: 23.2M), behind only last year.
It edged Yankees-Phillies Game 3 in 2009 (15.401M to 15.398M), which prior to last year was the most-watched Saturday game since ’04.
The World Series has averaged 15.4 million viewers through four games, down 15% from last year (18.2M), but up 10% from 2015 (13.98M).
