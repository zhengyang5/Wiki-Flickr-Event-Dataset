Houston Texans DE J.J. Watt -- on crutches -- threw out the ceremonial first pitch before the Astros' 5-3 win over the Los Angeles Dodgers in Game 3 of the World Series on Friday.
Watt has been on crutches since he broke his left leg on Oct. 8.
J.J. Watt received a huge ovation from the Houston crowd at Minute Maid Park before tossing the first pitch.
AP Photo/Matt SlocumWatt, who started a fundraiser that brought in more than $37 million for Hurricane Harvey relief efforts, received a huge ovation as he was introduced.
He made his way out to the mound before handing one of his crutches to girlfriend and Houston Dash soccer player Kealia Ohai.
