Here’s a look back at the first 18:1916: The first and so far only World Series meeting between the Dodgers and Red Sox.
1920: The Cleveland Indians have won just two World Series in their history; this one came at the Dodgers’ expense.
Sherry Smith pitched another complete game (Game 6) only to lose 1-0.
The Coliseum hosted Games 3-5, the first World Series games played on the West Coast.
Oakland returned to the World Series the following year; the Dodgers waited a generation.
