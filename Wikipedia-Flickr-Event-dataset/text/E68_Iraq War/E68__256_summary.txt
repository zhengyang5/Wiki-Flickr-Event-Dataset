Iraqi insurgents said Tuesday they killed a Bulgarian hostage and would put a second hostage to death in 24 hours, Al-Jazeera television reported.
Al-Jazeera broadcast a video of masked men standing behind Bulgarian hostage Georgi Lazov, 30.
Bulgaria identified the second hostage as Ivaylo Kepov.
Another Iraqi insurgent group holding an Egyptian hostage demanded the Saudi company he works for pull out of Iraq within 72 hours.
The fate of a Filipino hostage remains unclear after the Philippines issued a statement Monday saying they would pull out of Iraq.
