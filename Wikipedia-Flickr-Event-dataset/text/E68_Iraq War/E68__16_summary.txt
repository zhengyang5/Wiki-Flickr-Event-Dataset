Tomasz JuraFriday, May 04 2007 @ 06:15 AM MDTPoland.pl -- One Polish soldier has been killed and four others wounded in when a military vehicle hit a improvised explosive device (IED).
The incident took place in the northern part of ad Diwaniya, near to the Polish Echo base.
The victim was 25-year-old Private Tomasz Jura.
Spokesman of Poland's Defence Ministry Jaros_aw Rybak said that Private Jura was a volunteer, a machine-gun operator.
Since September 3, 2003, when the multinational division under Poland's command took over the central-southern stabilisation zone in Iraq, 25 Polish soldiers have lost their lives.
