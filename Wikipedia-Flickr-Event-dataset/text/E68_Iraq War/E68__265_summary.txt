The surest way to avoid attacks on Americans is to engage the enemy where he lives and plans.
We are fighting that enemy in Iraq and Afghanistan so that we do not meet him in our own cities.
Two years ago, the President told Congress and the country that the war on terror would be a lengthy war, fought on many fronts in many places.
The War on Terror in Afghanistan and Iraq will require a sustained commitment of time and resources, similar to our commitment to rebuilding Germany and Japan after World War II.
The President will soon submit to Congress a request of $87 billion for military and reconstruction efforts in Afghanistan and Iraq.
