LC: Statement by CS on "Consultation Document on the Methods for Selecting the Chief Executive in 2017 and for Forming the Legislative Council in 2016"************************************************************Following is the statement by the Chief Secretary for Administration, Mrs Carrie Lam, in the Legislative Council today (December 4) on the "Consultation Document on the Methods for Selecting the Chief Executive in 2017 and for Forming the Legislative Council in 2016":Mr President,Today, the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region (HKSAR) Government publishes the "Consultation Document on the Methods for Selecting the Chief Executive in 2017 and for Forming the Legislative Council in 2016" (Consultation Document) to formally commence a five-month public consultation exercise.
The overall objective is to maintain the long-term prosperity and stability of Hong Kong.
The ultimate aim of universal suffrage for the CE, as stipulated in the Basic Law, is just round the corner.
Today, we have formally stepped onto the straight road of welcoming universal suffrage.
This is also the constitutional responsibility of the HKSAR Government and a common aspiration of the people of Hong Kong.
