Several pro-democracy websites in Hong Kong were attacked this morning in a shadowy cyberblitz.
For over a century, Hong Kong was a colony of the British Empire.
The people of Hong Kong were legally guaranteed a high degree of autonomy, except in foreign and defense affairs.
Calls for greater freedom have emanated from Hong Kong for years.
Unfortunately, frustration with the Chinese government has, at times, translated to vitriol toward mainland Chinese visitors in Hong Kong.
