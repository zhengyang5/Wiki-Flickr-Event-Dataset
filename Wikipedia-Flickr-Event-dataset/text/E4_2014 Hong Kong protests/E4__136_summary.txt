How the past two months of protests have evolvedClashes erupted when a third protest site, at Mong Kok, was cleared last month.
At their height, the protests were seen as the biggest challenge to Beijing's rule in Hong Kong since the 1997 handover.
But numbers fell as weeks passed and many Hong Kong residents also spoke out against the protests because of disruption to the city.
