Thousands of migrants have landed on the Italian island of Lampedusa in recent weeks [Reuters]Around 350 migrants from Tunisia have arrived by boat overnight on an Italian island where authorities have declared a humanitarian emergency.
Thousands fleeing turmoil in North Africa, mostly from Tunisia, had already reached Lampedusa in the Mediterranean Sea before bad weather interrupted the flow last week.
Last month the Italian government said it planned to deploy security forces on Tunisian soil to stop illegal immigration.
