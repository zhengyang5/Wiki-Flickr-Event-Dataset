Ben Ali, second from left, visited the university graduate who's attempted suicide sparked the protests [AFP]Demonstrators in Tunisia are continuing their street protests, ignoring a warning by Zine al-Abidine Ben Ali, the Tunisian president, that disturbances will be firmly dealt with.
La Presse Tunisie, a Tunisian newspaper, reported on Wednesday that Ben Ali had visited Bouazizi in hospital.
The paper published a photo of Ben Ali at the young man's bedside.
Ben Ali has sought to restore calm to the north African Arab nation, promising new measures to create jobs.
Foreign media organisations have extremely limited access in the country.
