Tunisia President Turns Over Power to Prime MinisterBy Jihen Laghmari and Mahmoud Kassem(Updates with White House statement in 11th paragraph.)
Jan. 14 (Bloomberg) -- Tunisian Prime Minister Mohamed Ghannouchi assumed the duties of President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali after a declaration of a state of emergency prompted by demonstrations aimed at forcing Ben Ali’s resignation.
The French foreign ministry issued a statement that it has received no request from Ben Ali to go to France.
