The blast coincided with a renewed push by Russian-backed rebels to take Donetsk’s airport, in the north of the city, near Oktyabrsky.
At one time, Donetsk airport was considered a strategic point for both sides.
While the OSCE mission made two independent attempts to reach the airport, intense fighting caused them to turn back.
