Image copyright AFP Image caption Ukraine is confronting heavily armed rebels who have sworn allegiance to MoscowUkraine's defence minister says a new ceasefire has been holding in eastern Ukraine since midnight, despite a recent intensification of shelling.
The truce was agreed with pro-Russian rebels and international mediators.
Heightened tensionsEastern Ukraine has seen some of its worst violence for months in recent weeks, and there was heavy shelling last weekend.
The Kremlin denies deploying troops, but admits that Russian volunteers have been fighting alongside the rebels.
Despite the ceasefire agreed in Minsk in February 2015, there have been violations by both sides on an almost daily basis.
