Twenty federalization supporters have seized the local council building in Zhdanovka, Donetsk region, the Donbas News portal said on April 14. A flag of the "Donetsk republic" has been installed on the building, according to the portal. "No one resisted the attempt to seize the building. The organizer of the takeover has announced that Zhdanovka is now part of the Donetsk republic," the portal said.
