As Hurricane Irma bears down on the Caribbean and Florida, the governors of the Carolinas have declared states of emergency.
Roy Cooper on Wednesday declared a state of emergency, which will go into effect at 8 a.m. Thursday.
Cooper said the state is considering possible impacts to all 100 counties across the state.
