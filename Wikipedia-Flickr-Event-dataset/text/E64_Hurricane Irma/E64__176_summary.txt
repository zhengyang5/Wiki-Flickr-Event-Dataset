By Olivia RoseTHE TURKS and Caicos Islands along with several British overseas territories will now be eligible to receive official development aid.
Changes have been made to international rules which previously barred the territories from UK financial aid as they were deemed too rich.
These changes will now enable the Turks and Caicos Islands to benefit from a £13 billion relief fund to aid in the territory’s recovery.
BacklashThere was much controversy after it was revealed that Britain’s £13 billion aid budget could not be used to help repair the Islands.
