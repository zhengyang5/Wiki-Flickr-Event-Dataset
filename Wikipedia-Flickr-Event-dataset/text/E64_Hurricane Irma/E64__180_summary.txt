“NCP may not receive a vessel until late next week,” Mr Maura confirmed to Tribune Business via e-mail.
Hurricane Irma is expected to impact the port of Miami, Port Everglades, Port of Palm Beach and port of Jacksonville.
“It will have a pretty significant impact on our supply chain,” Mr Maura said of Irma.
“Hurricane Harvey devastated Texas and, as the rebuilding effort continues, building material supplies will be directed to Texas,” Mr Maura said.
