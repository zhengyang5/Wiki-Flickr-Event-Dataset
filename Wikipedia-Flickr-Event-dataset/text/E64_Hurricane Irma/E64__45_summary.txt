TALLAHASSEE, Fla. – Today, Governor Rick Scott received a full update on Hurricane Irma from the State Emergency Operations Center.
Miami-Dade – mandatory evacuations for all of Zone A, all of Zone B, and portions of Zone C.Monroe – mandatory evacuations for visitors and residents.
Sarasota – mandatory evacuations in Zone A (Longboat, Lido, Siesta, Casey Key, Manasota Key, Venice Island), all mobile homesSeminole – mandatory evacuations for mobile and manufactured homes.
STATE EMERGENCY OPERATIONS CENTER/ CONTACT INFORMATIONThe State Emergency Operations Center remains activated at a level one, which is a full-scale, 24-hours-a-day activation.
The Florida Emergency Information Line has been activated and is available 24/7 for families and victors at 1-800-342-3557.
