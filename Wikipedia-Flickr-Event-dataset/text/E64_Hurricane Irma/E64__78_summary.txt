Hurricane Irma leaves St. Kitts and Nevis with initial $53.2 million in damagesFrom SKNISBasseterre, St. Kitts – Hurricane Irma left a trail of destruction in its wake in the Caribbean.
While the damages in St. Kitts and Nevis were minor compared to other islands that were almost completely devastated, the twin-island federation is in recuperation from the damages it suffered.
“The estimate of damage to St Kitts and Nevis has been conservatively put at EC$53.2 million,” he revealed.
On the island of St. Kitts, our estimate is EC$41 million.
St Kitts and Nevis was spared a direct hit and consequential loss of lives.
