MIAMI (September 6, 2017) - The upcoming Miami FC road match against the San Francisco Deltas on Saturday, September 9 has been postponed.
Incoming Hurricane Irma has forced Miami FC's players and technical staff to prepare themselves and their families for the effects and aftermath of a hurricane of this magnitude.
There is yet to be a new date announced for the postponed match, but we will update everyone once a new match date is finalized.
