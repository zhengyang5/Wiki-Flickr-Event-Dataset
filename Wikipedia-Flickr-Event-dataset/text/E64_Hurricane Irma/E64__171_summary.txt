They are providing advice and support to British nationals in Florida and issuing emergency travel documents.
They have provided medical supplies and aid including 2.2 tonnes of UK aid shelter kits providing shelter for almost 1,000 people, rations and clean water.
A total of 10,000 UK aid buckets and 5,000 solar lanterns have been dispatched.
HMS Ocean is also being deployed and will help with the reconstruction after the hurricane.
Additional UK government staff are on standby to provide assistance once the hurricane has passed.
