Press Release:-According to the Saint Lucia Meteorological Service, as at 11:00 am on Monday September 04, 2017, the eye of Hurricane Irma was located near latitude 16.8 north, longitude 53.3 west or about 560 miles or 905 kilometres east of the Leeward Islands.
Irma is moving toward the west-southwest near 14 mph or 22 km/h but a turn toward the west is forecast later today.
Irma is a category 3 hurricane with maximum sustained winds near 120 mph or 195 km/h, with higher gusts and some additional strengthening is forecast through Tuesday night.
On the projected track, the center of Hurricane Irma is expected to move near or over portions of the northern Leeward Islands Tuesday night and early Wednesday.
Prepare an emergency plan and discuss this plan with your family and employees.
