Article Borough Market bounces back Categories: News and previewsOn Wednesday morning, Borough Market reopened after a simple, poignant ceremony.
Donald Hyslop, the chair of Trustees of Borough Market, reflects on an important step along the road to recoveryYesterday morning, something resembling normality returned to Borough Market.
Suléy Muhidin read his poem Keeping Tradition Alive, which was inspired by his love of Borough, then a group of our traders rang the market bell.
And that was that: Borough Market was back in business.
