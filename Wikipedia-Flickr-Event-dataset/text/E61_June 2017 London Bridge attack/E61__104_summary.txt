4 June 2017Harun Khan, Secretary General of the Muslim Council of Britain has issued the following statement after the terrorist incident in London:“I am appalled and angered by the terrorist attacks at London Bridge and Borough Market, in my home city.
These acts of violence were truly shocking and I condemn them in the strongest terms.
Muslims everywhere are outraged and disgusted at these cowards who once again have destroyed the lives of our fellow Britons.
I commend the work of our emergency services working hard to keep us safe and cope with the ensuing carnage.
As ever we urge everyone to assist the authorities so that these criminals can be apprehended and brought to justice.”
