Viktor Orbán has expressed his shock and condolences to British Prime Minister Theresa May with regard to the terrorist attacks in London on Saturday, the Prime Minister’s Press Chief, Bertalan Havasi informed the kormany.hu government news portal.

“The attacks on Great Britain are particularly shocking to all Hungarians in view of the fact that in recent decades many of our compatriots have chosen your beautiful capital as a place to work or study”, Mr. Orbán wrote, stressing that Hungary’s government and the people of Hungary “remain committed to contributing to the protection of our common security”. “I hereby express my deepest sympathy to the families of the victims and wish the injured a speedy recovery”, the Prime Minister’s letter states in closing.
