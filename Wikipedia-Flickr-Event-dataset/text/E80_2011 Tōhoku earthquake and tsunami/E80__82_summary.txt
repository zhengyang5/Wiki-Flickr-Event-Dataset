Tsunami Aftermath in Galapagos: Update from CDRS' Director Dr. J. Gabriel Lopez14 March 2011Reports continue to come in from our colleagues in Galapagos.
Below is a letter from the director of the Charles Darwin Research Station, which describes some of the damage caused by the tsunami of March 11, 2011.
_______________________________________________________________The tsunami waves were far more intense than during last year’s tsunami alert.
Wave amplitude at Santa Cruz was 1.77 meters (nearly 6 ft) and the arrival time of the tsunami waves coincided with the high tide.
The Biomar Building at the Charles Darwin Research Station suffered serious damage in Friday’s tsunami.
