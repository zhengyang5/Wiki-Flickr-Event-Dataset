The boy’s 33-year-old mother was swept away by the massive tsunami while working in the neighboring town of Otsuchi, also in Iwate.
“Sora lost his mother and we lost our daughter, and it is just too much to bear,” she said.
Then one night the grandmother told him the truth.
While the grandparents were being interviewed, the boy at times mimicked his schoolteachers and actors in TV dramas, bursting into laughter.
His grandmother suffered a fall in late October and broke her wrist, and the boy has since helped even more with daily chores.
