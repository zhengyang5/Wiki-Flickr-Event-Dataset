Information message about destructive earthquake near the eastern coast of Honshu Island of Japana on March 11, 2011.
Destructive earthquake with M=8.8 has occurred near the eastern coast of Honshu Island of Japan on March 11, 2011 at 05:46 GMT (08:46 Moscow time).
