Liberal MP Alby Schultz is set to retire at the next federal election.
Mr Schultz said today it was time to "move on", despite wanting to continue to serve his electorate in the rural NSW seat of Hume.
Liberal MP Alby Schultz Photo: Dave Tease"As the longest serving Member for Hume since Federation, it is with mixed feelings that I have reached this conclusion," he said, in a statement.
Mr Schultz, 72, has previously said there had been attempts to push him aside from his safe seat by Liberal and National Party figures.
The Member for Hume, based in Goulburn, has also been sending mixed messages about his future plans.
