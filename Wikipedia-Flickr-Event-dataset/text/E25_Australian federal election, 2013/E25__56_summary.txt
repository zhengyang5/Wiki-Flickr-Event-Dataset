Veteran Queensland Nationals senator Ron Boswell has announced he won't renominate for the Senate in 2014.
Senator Boswell is currently the longest-serving member of the Senate, having been elected in May 1983.
He served as the leader of the Nationals in the Senate from 1990 to 2007.
Senator Boswell has never served in a ministry during his career, but was parliamentary secretary for transport from 1999 to 2003.Nationals leader Warren Truss said Senator Boswell is ‘‘one of the parliament’s best known and best loved characters’’.
‘‘Ron has been, and remains, a courageous and unwavering champion for Queensland - especially for the regions, their people and their industries.’’
