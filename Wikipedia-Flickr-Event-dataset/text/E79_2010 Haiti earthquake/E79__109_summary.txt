BY KING QUILLEN Before her January journey to Haiti, Mary Ann Loafman would not speculate about the destruction she would find.
The Holy Name of Jesus team visited what remained of two mountain parishes it supports, Les Palmes and Morne a Chandelle.
Her husband, Jim, called the destruction “overwhelming.” About 64,000 people lived in the three agricul- tural parishes.
