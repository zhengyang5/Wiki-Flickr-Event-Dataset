PokerStars will match all donations, whichever method you choose to give, and send the total to the Red Cross.
On Full Tilt Poker, players can register for dummy tournaments, as well.
Players may also choose to donate privately by transferring funds to ‘Aid for Haiti’ on the site.
Cake Poker is also matching its players’ donations and has formed three dummy tournaments — at $5, $25, and $100.
As for DoylesRoom, the site will be donating all of its tournament fees from this Wednesday’s celebrity poker tournament, which takes place at 9:30 p.m.
