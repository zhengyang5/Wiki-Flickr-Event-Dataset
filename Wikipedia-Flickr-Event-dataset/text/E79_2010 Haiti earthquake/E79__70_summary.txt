[Photo:Xinhua/Xing Guangli]Special Coverage: Haiti EarthquakeThe 50-member Chinese International Rescue Team has added its weight to the international aid efforts in Haiti in the wake of a magnitude 7.3 earthquake.
The Chinese rescue team arrived at Port-au-Prince Thursday morning with three rescue dogs and some 20 tons of equipment and humanitarian aids.
Chinese team leader Huang Jianfa said although Chinese experts worked all day on Thursday, hopes were fading fast.
According to Chinese business envoy in Haiti Wang Shuping, 18 people working for Chinese companies in Haiti have been rescued.
The Chinese relief team was amongst the first on the ground in Haiti.
