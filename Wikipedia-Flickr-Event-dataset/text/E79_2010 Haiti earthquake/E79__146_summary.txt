Status: CompletedSummaryFollowing the January 12th, earthquake in Haiti in 2010, RMF focused on providing volunteer medical staffing, in-kind medical supplies and strategic coordination to help meet the surging needs of the health crisis on the ground.
RMF also provided direct funding, medical supplies and pharmaceuticals to local health facilities and partner hospitals, as well as advisory services and coordination to local health facilities and coordinated mobile health outreaches, field clinics and food supplies to outlying villages overlooked in the relief effort.
