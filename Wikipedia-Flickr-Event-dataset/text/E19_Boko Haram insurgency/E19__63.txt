At least 329 schoolgirls — more than three times as many as first thought — were kidnapped from their dormitories by Boko Haram a fortnight ago, their head teacher said yesterday.

Asabe Kwambura said that police in the town of Chibok in Borno state had been forced to dramatically revise the number of pupils who had been abducted, because many of them had come from neighbouring areas to sit exams.
