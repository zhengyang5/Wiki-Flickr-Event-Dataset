Parliamentary Representative for the Petite Savanne Constituency, Dr. Kenneth Darroux has appealed to members of the public to be sensitive to the plight of those displaced as a result of Tropical Storm Erika.
Today, it’s Petite Savanne, tomorrow, it could be their community.
