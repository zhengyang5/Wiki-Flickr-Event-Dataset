Government has identified several locations to permanently resettle those displaced by Tropical Storm Erika.
Prime Minister Roosevelt Skerrit made the announcement at a press conference on Friday, October 9th.
He listed, “Warner; Geneva, Grand Bay; near the Pierre Charles Secondary School in Grand Bay; Bellevue Chopin, and La Plaine.
“ADRA has offered us, in the first instance, 10 two and three bedroom homes.
Five million dollars approved by the Mexican Government for the indoor sporting facility has been reallocated to resettlement efforts.
