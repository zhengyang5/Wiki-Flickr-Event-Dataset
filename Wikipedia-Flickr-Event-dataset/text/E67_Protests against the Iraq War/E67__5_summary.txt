Student protesters helped drive Lyndon Johnson — in so many ways a powerful, progressive president — out of office because of his war.
In 2004, George W. Bush — in so many ways a weak, regressive president — was re-elected despite his war.
And antiwar protesters are told they’re un-American, cowardly and lending aid and comfort to terrorists.
But in the 1960’s and 1970’s, antiwar protesters were told they were un-American, cowardly and lending aid and comfort to Communists.
Now, people find protesters vaguely embarrassing and don’t want to make too much noise.
