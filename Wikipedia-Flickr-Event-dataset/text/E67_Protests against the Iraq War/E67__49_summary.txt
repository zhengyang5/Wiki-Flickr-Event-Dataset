On Wednesday 9th August Anti War protesters in Derry stormed into the local plant of US missile manufacturer Raytheon.
They were protesting against the use of Raytheon technology by the Israelis and used by them in their attacks in Lebanon.
IMC Blogger: Both the IAWM and AWI issued quick statements of support as part of the ongoing rapprochement that is within the anti war movements on Indy these days.
