PML-N member claims that he person­ally spoke with three PTI senior leader­sISLAMABAD: Addressing a press conference outside the Parliament House in Islamabad, Minister for Railways Khawaja Saad Rafique maintained a fine balance between conciliatory politics and defiance.
As part of the PML-N’s continuing diplomatic efforts to defuse the political crisis, Rafique, who belongs to PML-N, claimed that he personally spoke with three PTI senior leaders, namely Arif Alvi, Asad Umar, Shah Mehmood Qureshi.
He did not disclose the minutes of the conversation, but did maintain that negotiations are ongoing.
At the same time, he warned that marching into the Red Zone will have consequences.
