A twenty-year-old man who had been watching the Boston Marathon had his body torn into by the force of a bomb.
After the bombs went off, people were running in every direction—so was the young man.
In the midst of that, according to a CBS News report, a bystander saw the young man running, badly hurt, rushed to him, and then “tackled him,” bringing him down.
“Investigators have a suspect—a Saudi Arabian national—in the horrific Boston Marathon bombings, The Post has learned.” That’s the New York Post, which went on to cite Fox News.
