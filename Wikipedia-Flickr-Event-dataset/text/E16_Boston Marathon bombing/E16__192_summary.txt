More information emerges on the three friends who aided the Boston Marathon bombing suspect, Dzhokar Tsarnaev.
Robel Phillipos, 19, appeared in federal court in Boston on May 1 after he was charged with lying to investigators about marathon bombing suspect Dzhokhar Tsarnaev.
Phillipos, Tsarnaev and Dias Kadyrbayev and Azamat Tazhayakov attended the University of Massachusetts-Dartmouth.
Phillipos, 19, of Cambridge, was studying marketing, but the university said Wednesday he is not currently enrolled.
The Federal Bureau of Investigation shows the remnants of a pressure cooker they say was part of one of the bombs used during the Boston Marathon on April 15.
