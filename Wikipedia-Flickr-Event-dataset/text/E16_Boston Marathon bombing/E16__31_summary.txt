The American Red Cross issued the following statement on the explosions at the Boston Marathon:Our hearts go out to all those in Boston dealing with this tragedy and our thoughts are with all of those injured.
The Red Cross has sent more than 100 additional blood products to several Boston area hospitals to help meet patient needs.
People can call 1-800-RED CROSS or go to redcrossblood.org to schedule an appointment to give blood in the days and weeks ahead.
Over the next few hours, the Red Cross will be working in close coordination with officials to determine how we can best help the community and support emergency workers.
Many Red Cross volunteers and workers were on the marathon course, as they are every year to support the runners.
