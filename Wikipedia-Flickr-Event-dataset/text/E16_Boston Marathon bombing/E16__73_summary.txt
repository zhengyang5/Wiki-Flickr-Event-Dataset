Simply speaking, a series of violent events culminated in Watertown, Massachusetts late Thursday night after two unidentified suspects engaged with police from a Mercedes SUV.
As many as 100 police officers faced off against the two suspects, who were armed with explosives and firearms.
While fleeing, the suspects in the SUV lost control and crashed the vehicle and made their way out of the situation by tossing a bomb towards authorities.
The bomb exploded, and the suspects continued to flee, later dropping backpacks that police fear may contain explosives.
