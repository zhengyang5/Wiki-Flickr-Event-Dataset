Thousands of soldiers voted before the evening.
Follow Ynetnews on Facebook and TwitterA total of 668 ballot boxes have been set up for IDF soldiers.
Conscripts and reservists can vote at any polling station located at an army base, and, as in previous elections, there will be mobile polling stations traveling between distant army posts to allow those serving there to vote.
Photo: AFPSoldiers who are on leave on the day of elections are allowed to vote at polling stations set up in military offices and will not be required to vote in uniform.
According to the lines witnessed at the naval base in Haifa on Sunday, it appeared that there would be a high voter turnout among IDF soldiers.
