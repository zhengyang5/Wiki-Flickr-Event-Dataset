The Knesset plenum voted on Thursday in a vote of 61-59 in favor of Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu's cabinet, paving the way for the swearing in of Israel's 34th government.
The Knesset deliberations were meant to begin at 7 P.M. on Thursday, but were postponed due to disagreements over the portfolios.
The prime minister chose to keep the communication ministry and the foreign ministry portfolio, the latter possibly to be used later to entice the Zionist Union into the coalition.
