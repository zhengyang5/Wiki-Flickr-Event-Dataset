Netanyahu says no bad blood with ObamaIn his first interview since reelection, Netanyahu rejected the notion that his reelection and the stormy campaign will leave their mark on Israel-American relations.
“I think there’s an unbreakable bond between Israel and the United States; the president said that, I said that….” says Netanyahu in an interview to MSNBC that will be aired this evening.
We’ll work together, we have to.
We have our differences on Iran.
America has no greater ally than Israel and Israel has no greater ally than the United States.”
