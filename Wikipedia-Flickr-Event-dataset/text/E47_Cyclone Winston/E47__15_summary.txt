/ Front page / NewsUpdate: 7:20PM THE authorities are yet to get updates from Vanuabalavu in Lau because of communication problems.
The National Disaster Management Office confirmed that efforts made to get in touch with the people proved futile as all means of communication on the island is down.
Vanuabalavu was among the first islands to be hit by Severe Tropical Cyclone Winston on Saturday morning.
A Government team is expected to be deployed to the island this week.
