Ratu Inoke Kubuabola received the donation from Vanuatu Foreign Minister Bruno Leingkone who presented it on behalf of the Vanuatu Council of Ministers and the people of Vanuatu.
Minister Leingkone said Fiji would always be a close friend, a brother and an important development partner to Vanuatu.
Minister Kubuabola said the people of Fiji were grateful and humbled by the gratitude expressed by the people of Vanuatu.
Minister Kubuabola said the assistance from international development partners especially their bilateral partners was overwhelming.
