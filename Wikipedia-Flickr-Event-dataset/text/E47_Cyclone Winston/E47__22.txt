12 villagers injured in Nabuna, Koro

15:36 Sun Feb 21, 2016

Taken from/By:

Report by: Tokasa Rainima

Twelve people have been injured in the village of Nabuna in Koro Island in the aftermath of TC Winston.

School teacher of Vunivasa District school, Temo Nacolaivalu says some villagers were hit by flying debris and they need assistance.

“ There is a lot of damage in the school including the teacher’s quarters and roofs of some homes were blown away’’.

He said the villagers are using one classroom as an evacuation centre.
