Nationwide Curfew as TC Winston batters Fiji group17:02 Sat Feb 20, 2016Taken from/By:Report by: Indra SinghA nationwide curfew has been imposed as Severe Tropical Cyclone Winston continues to affect the country.
The curfew, which starts at 6pm will be in place until further notice.
Meanwhile, strong winds and heavy rain is now being experienced in Navua.
Although no damages have been reported so far, Police are closely monitoring the situation.
The river level has not reached alert levels so far with high tide occurring in the last hour.
