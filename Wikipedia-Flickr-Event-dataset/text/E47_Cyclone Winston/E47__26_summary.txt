/ Front page / NewsUpdate: 6:46PM PEOPLE living in Nausori have started to face the rage of Hurricane Winston.
Confirmations have been received that two houses have blown away in Waidamu, just outside Nausori Town by the destructive winds.
Resident, Navinesh Joshep said the Waidamu River has burst its bank and low-lying areas were getting flooded.
He said the destructive winds has damaged his garage as well.
