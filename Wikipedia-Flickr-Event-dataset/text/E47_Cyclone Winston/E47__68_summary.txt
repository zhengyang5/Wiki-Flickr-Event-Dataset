The Pacific State of Fiji has begun the arduous task of cleaning up after the strongest ever storm in the Southern Hemisphere.
Damage from Storm Winston is reportedly extensive in the East, power is out over most of the country, and a curfew is in place.
IOM is preparing to assist as needed and has allocated USD 200,000 from its Migration Emergency Funding Mechanism to help with immediate disaster assessments and response.
“Our power and water are back – Suva’s services have largely been restored and thankfully everyone around here seems fine,” she added.
