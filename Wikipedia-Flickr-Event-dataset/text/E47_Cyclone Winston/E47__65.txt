Update: 4:00PM THE Fiji Red Cross Society launched an appeal today to provide support to people affected by Severe Tropical Cyclone Winston.

FRCS national president Cathy Wong says the most vulnerable and informal settlements are the top priority of disaster relief response.

Cash donations would be used to facilitate the distribution of shelter kits, clothes, blankets, cooking sets and hygiene kits.

Donations can be made to any ANZ Bank branch.
