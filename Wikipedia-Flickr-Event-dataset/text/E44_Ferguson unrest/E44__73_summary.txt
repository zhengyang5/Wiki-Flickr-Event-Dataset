(Joe Raedle/Getty Images)Twelve days after Darren Wilson fatally shot 18-year-old Michael Brown, sparking protests and riots in the town of Ferguson, Mo., supporters have taken to the Internet to raise funds for him.
The description: “We stand behind Officer Darren Wilson and his family during this trying time in their lives.
There is a “Support Darren Wilson” Facebook community page that has amassed more than 50,000 likes and is selling shirts and hoodies on teespring.com.
They feature a badge emblem that reads: “Officer Darren Wilson.
Internet users are raising funds in the name of Michael Brown as well.
