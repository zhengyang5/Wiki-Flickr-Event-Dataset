(AP) - A Ferguson convenience store that was looted and burned after the fatal shooting of Michael Brown will be rebuilt as a job training center.
The Urban League of Metropolitan St. Louis said Friday that the center is part of its "empowering communities" effort.
The St. Louis Post-Dispatch reports the center will house the new Save Our Sons program.
