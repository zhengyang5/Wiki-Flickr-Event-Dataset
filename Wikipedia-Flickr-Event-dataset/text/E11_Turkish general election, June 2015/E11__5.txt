Opposition Nationalist Movement Party (MHP) has filed a criminal complaint against the İstanbul Metropolitan Municipality (ibb), claiming that municipal police tore down some of party’s election campaign banners.

MHP İstanbul Provincial Branch Chairman Mehmet Bülent Karataş claimed on Monday that MHP’s campaign banners hang in the Kadıköy district ahead of the June 7 parliamentary elections were torn down by İBB’s municipal police and that the party filed a criminal complaint at the İstanbul Public Prosecutor’s Office against the İBB over the incident.

The İstanbul Municipality is run by the ruling Justice and Development Party (AK Party).
