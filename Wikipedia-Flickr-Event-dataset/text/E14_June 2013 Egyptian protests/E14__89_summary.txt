Protesters gathered in Alexandria, Minya, Menufiya, Suez, Daqahleya, Beni Suef, Port Said, and in Tanat and Mahalla in the governorate of Gharbeya on Sunday.
In Mansoura, Daqahleya hundreds of thousands of protesters gathered.
Mohamed Al-Mohandes from Mansoura claimed that the number even exceeded a million protesters.
“Security makes sure that no one gets in with weapons, party banners or demands that are different than those of Tamarod,” Al-Mohandes said.
Meanwhile in Suez, thousands took part in a march demanding that Morsi leave because of his failure to govern effectively.
