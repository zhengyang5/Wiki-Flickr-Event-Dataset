Three Bombers Attacked the StadiumThree attackers detonated suicide vests outside the Stade de France, where a soccer match between France and Germany was being played.
Gate D BomberThe first attacker detonated his explosives at 9:20 p.m. on Rue Rimet, near Gate D of the stadium.
The attacker was carrying a passport for a 25-year-old named Ahmad al-Mohammad from Idlib, Syria.
Gate H BomberThe unidentified second attacker at the stadium detonated his explosives at 9:30 p.m. on Rue Rimet, near Gate H of the stadium.
Officials reported on Friday that fingerprints matching the bomber were taken at a check for migrants in Greece on Oct. 3.
