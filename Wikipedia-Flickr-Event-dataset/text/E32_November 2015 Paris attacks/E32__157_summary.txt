– ‘In this test, only the unity will defeat this barbaric terrorism’, Union of Islamic Organizations of France saysPARIS – Several French Muslim organizations have firmly condemned the Paris attacks that left 129 people dead and 352 injured Friday night.
The French Council of the Muslim Faith (CFCM) condemned “strongly these odious and despicable attacks”.
The president of the Great Mosque of Paris, Dalil Boubake, expressed his “greatest condemnation of the terrible murderous wave of terrorist attacks”.
The Collective Against Islamophobia in France issued a statement saying that Muslims “refuse to give in to fear despite the unprecedented nature of the tragedy.
– US Muslim groups join condemnationsMuslim American organizations too condemned the attacks Saturday, saying they don’t represent the Islamic faith.
