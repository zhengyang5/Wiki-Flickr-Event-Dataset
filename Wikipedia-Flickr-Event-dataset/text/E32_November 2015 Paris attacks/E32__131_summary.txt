In India, TV channels would have gone berserk screaming the usual clichéd nonsense – remember the “spirit of Mumbai”.
In Paris, the man with the peace sign painted on his piano, quietly ended his spellbinding concerto, dabbed away tears from his eyes and walked away to quiet applause from the media scrum.
Watching coverage of the Paris attacks, the comparisons with 26/11 in Mumbai are striking.
It is also true that the sophisticated command and control system used by the Mumbai attackers with their overseas handlers and their desire to prolong the fighting seemed relatively missing in Paris.
France has called the Paris mayhem an “act of war”.
