Police enlist down-and-outs to destroy tents and lash out at journalists filming the proceedingsThe police in Odessa have forcibly removed the tents erected on Prymorsky Boulevard by protesters.
The court ruling places a blanket ban on all demonstrations, the use of tents, sound equipment and vehicles until the end of the year.
They were especially antagonistic towards journalists filming what was going on.
What remained of the tents was put on a GAZel vehicle without number plates and taken away.
The police detained three of the protesters, including the leader of the Odessa branch of Democratic Alliance, Alexei Chorny.
