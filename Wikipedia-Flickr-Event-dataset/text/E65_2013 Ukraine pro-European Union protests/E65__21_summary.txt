The leader of “Svoboda” Oleh Tiagnybok believes that "Berkut" must be disbanded - he said this at a briefing, reports UNN.
He believes that the law enforcement officers involved in the beating protesters should be prosecuted.
Tyagnybok also announced once again about provocations during protests: According Tiagnybok, they involved "5000" titushky".
He added that the capital is being tightened by new "titushky".
