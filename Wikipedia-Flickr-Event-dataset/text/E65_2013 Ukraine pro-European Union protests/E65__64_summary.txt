Pro-European demonstrators continued their round-the-clock protest, angry at Viktor Yanukovich for shelving the planned association agreement.
Less than a kilometre away, a counter-demonstration formed in support of the decision to walk away from the EU deal.
“We are not against an association deal with the EU, just not at any price.
The right moment has not come yet – there is a right time for everything,” said a middle-aged man.
We think European integration is not necessary for us at the moment, so we came to support the president,” another man added.
