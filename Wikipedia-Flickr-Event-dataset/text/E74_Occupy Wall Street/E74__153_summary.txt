51% See Occupy Wall Street Protesters As Public NuisanceEnough is enough as far as most voters are concerned when it comes to the Occupy Wall Street protesters.
Voters now view the protesters as a public nuisance.
The latest Rasmussen Reports national telephone survey also finds that just 24% think the Occupy Wall Street protesters who first began their protests in early October have gotten their message across to the American people.
The national survey of 1,000 Likely Voters was conducted on January 3-4, 2012 by Rasmussen Reports.
Field work for all Rasmussen Reports surveys is conducted by Pulse Opinion Research, LLC.
