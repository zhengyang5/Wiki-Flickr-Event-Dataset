Khalid Sheikh Mohammed Terror Indictment Unsealed, DismissedBy Bob Van Voris and Patricia Hurtado(Updates with specific charges in third paragraph.)
Mohammed was previously named in an indictment filed against his nephew for the 1993 World Trade Center bombing case.
The nephew, Ramzi Yousef, was convicted at a trial of orchestrating the first World Trade Center bombing.
The case is U.S. v. Mohammed, 93-cr-180, U.S. District Court, Southern District of New York (Manhattan).
