World Trade Center Re-opens as Tallest Building in AmericaNovember 3rd 2014By Jack Moore - IB TimesThirteen years after the 9/11 attacks tore down the Twin Towers, the new One World Trade Center skyscraper is reopening on a milestone day for the American city.
"The New York City skyline is whole again, as One World Trade Center takes its place in LowerManhattan," said Patrick Foye, executive director of the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey, the owner of the skyscraper and the construction site.
The opening of the building marks the rehabilitation of the lower Manhattan region which was devastated by the 9/11 attacks in 2001.
Its opening comes after the 9/11 memorial opened on 11 September 2011 and the 9/11 Museum was opened six months ago.
