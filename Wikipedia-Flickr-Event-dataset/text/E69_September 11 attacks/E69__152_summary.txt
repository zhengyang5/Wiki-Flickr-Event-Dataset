You can also contact the WTC Health Program at 1-888-982-4748 for information on how to apply.
If you are not yet enrolled, and believe you may be eligible for the Program, please visit the WTC Health Program website for information about the application process, www.cdc.gov/wtc or contact the WTC Health Program at 1-888-982-4748.
All care for the WTC Health Program must be rendered by a provider affiliated with the WTC Health Program.
The VCF is administered by the Department of Justice, and enrollment in that program is separate from the WTC Health Program.
However, the James Zadroga 9/11 Health and Compensation Act only provides funding for the WTC Health Program through 2016.
