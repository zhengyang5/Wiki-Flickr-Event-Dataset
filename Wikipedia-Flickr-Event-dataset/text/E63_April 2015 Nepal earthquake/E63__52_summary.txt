A Melbourne woman in Nepal for a Mount Everest trekking expedition has been confirmed dead, among more than 3000 people killed in the massive earthquake.
Counsellor Renu Fotedar was among those who had been killed in an avalanche on the mountain.
At least 19 people at the Mount Everest base camp were killed in the avalanche, triggered by the quake.
On her website, Ms Fotedar described herself as a transpersonal counsellor who had worked throughout India, the Middle East, Europe and Australia.
Ms Fotedar, who lived in Melbourne, grew up in Kashmir, in India's north.
