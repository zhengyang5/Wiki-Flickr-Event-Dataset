Two planes that carried Israelis rescued from the earthquake in Nepal landed in Israel on Monday.
Five other flights flew to Kathmandu on Monday with Israeli rescue teams, IDF soldiers and medical personnel to help Nepal, which is still devastated by the 7.9 magnitude quake that struck on Saturday, killing over 3,300 people.
