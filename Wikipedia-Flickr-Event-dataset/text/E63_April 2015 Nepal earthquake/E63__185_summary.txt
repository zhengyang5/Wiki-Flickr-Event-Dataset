The great earthquake hit the Nepali capital Katmandu at 11:56, April 25 in local time.
Within only half day, Japanese organisations have already started emergency assistance.
We all know how hard the earthquake is, and our experts know what will happen next and what people therefore will need.
Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) is sending 70 experts departing April 26 from Narita Airport.
Association of Medical Doctors of Asia (AMDA) and Shapla Neer also announced on SNS that they had started coordination towards their emergency assistance.
