(Source: Associated Press)Nepal was struck by a devastating 7.9 magnitude earthquake on Saturday, one of the worst to have hit the country since 1934.
In 1934, an earthquake had hit Nepal and Bihar and over 19,000 people were killed in total.
In 2015, the earthquake was felt as far as New Delhi, Kolkata and has left over 1800 people dead so far in Nepal itself.
