“The Philippine-MMDA Humanitarian Assistance Team has moved to Dadhuwa, Ramechhap, to give medical assistance to people in the village as part of the task allocated by Nepal’s Ministry of Health.

MMDA Chair Francis Tolentino earlier said that the MMDA rescue and retrieval team was sent to Nepal to help the country in rescue and recovery efforts.
