India has evacuated 170 people of 15 countries including the US, the UK, Russia and Germany, External Affairs Ministry said today.
India assisted in evacuation from Nepal of 170 nationals from 15 countries via commercial & IAF aircraft," Spokesperson in the External Affairs Ministry Vikas Swarup tweeted.
Nepal suffered one of the worst earthquakes on Saturday when it was hit by the 7.9-magnitude temblor in which death toll has been put at around 10,000 by Prime Minister Sushil Koirala.
India is involved in massive rescue and relief efforts in the Himalayan country.
The government has also brought back over 5,400 Indians so far.
