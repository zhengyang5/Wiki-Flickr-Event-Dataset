Apr 28, 2015-The death toll from the Great Earthquake has reached 4,680 and the number is counting.
The highest number of casualty has been reported from Central Development Region with the toll reaching 2,797.
Similarly, 258 deaths have been reported from Western Region, 51 from Eastern Region and two from Mid-Western Region.
Owing to inclement weather on Tuesday, the NA and NDRF could only airlift 138 injured victims to the Capital from various earthquake-hit districts.
The NA and NDRF have been carrying out search and rescue operation using 12 helicopters.
