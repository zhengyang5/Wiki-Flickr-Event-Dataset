Jun 11, 2015-The death toll from the landslide triggered by incessant rainfall in Taplejung has reached 53.
The entire area of Liwang, Thokling, Thinglabu and Lingket VDCs have been reportedly severely hit by the mudslip triggered by rainfall.
Some wards of Khalung and Khokling VDCs have also been affected.
Likewise, more than two dozen houses in the six VDCs are reported to have been washed away by the landslide.
The landslide in Myankha of the district last night has also obstructed the Mechi highway.
