A Massive Earthquake was experienced in several states of North India including Bihar, West Bengal, Jharkhand, Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Rajasthan and Delhi.
The epicentre of Earthquake was located 29 km (18mi) ESE of Lamjung, Nepal where it was recorded 7.9 on Richter scale, reports United States Geological Survey.
