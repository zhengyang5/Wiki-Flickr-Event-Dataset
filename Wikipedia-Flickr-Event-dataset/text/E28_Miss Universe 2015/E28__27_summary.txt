Narissara Nena France was crowned Miss Universe Great Britain 2015 at the end of the pageant held on June 27, 2015.
She will have a few months to prepare for Miss Universe 2015, where she hopes to be crowned by the reigning Miss Universe, Paulina Vega from Colombia.
