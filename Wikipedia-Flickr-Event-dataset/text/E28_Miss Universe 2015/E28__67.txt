Twenty-two-year-old Lisa Marie White has been crowned Miss Universe Singapore 2015.



The aspiring actress, who is of Malay and Kiwi descent, was chosen by a private jury on Friday and revealed to the public for the first time at prestigious F1 after-party Amber Lounge on Saturday (Sep 19).



The evening also saw performances by Apl.de.ap of The Black Eyed Peas, hip hop star Eve and LMFAO’s Sky Blue, as well as F1 drivers such as Williams’ Adrian Sutil and Ferrari’s Esteban Gutierrez suiting up in Ted Baker’s AW15 collection for the F1 Driver Fashion Show.
