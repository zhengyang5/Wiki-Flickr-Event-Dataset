After a national casting held on November 25, Giada Pezzaioli was chosen to represent Italy at the Miss Universe contest.
The pageant will be held in Las Vegas, Nevada with the finals on December 20.
She represented Italy at Miss World 2010 where she was one of the Top 7.
Italy is currently ranked 26th at the Big4 Ranking[CLICK HERE] and surprisingly, without a Miss Universe crown.
At the Miss Universe 2014 pageant held last January 2015, Italy – represented by Valentina Bonariva – made it to the semis.
