Dasa Radosavljevic, finished as first runner-up in the just concluded Mis Srbije 2014.
The 18 year old beauty from Kragujevac will represent Serbia at the Miss Universe 2015.
The pageant on January 25, 2015 is considered as Miss Universe 2014.Miss Universe is an annual international beauty contest that is run by the Miss Universe Organization.
Along with the Miss Earth and Miss World contests, Miss Universe is one of the three largest beauty pageants in the world in terms of the number of national-level competitions to participate in the world finals.
