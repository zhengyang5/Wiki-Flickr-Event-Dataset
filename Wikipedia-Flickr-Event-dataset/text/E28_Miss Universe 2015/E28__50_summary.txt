At the conclusion of the finals, Denisa Vyšnovská from Prievidze was adjudged the winner , and was crowned Miss Universe Slovenskej Republiky 2015.
Her court included Anita Polgáriová from Roznava, the first runner-up and Miss Earth Slovakia 2015, and Dominika Zemanová from Bratislava, the second runner-up and Miss Intercontinental Slovakia 2015.
The runners-up will go on to compete in Miss Earth 2015, and Miss Intercontinental 2015 respectively.
