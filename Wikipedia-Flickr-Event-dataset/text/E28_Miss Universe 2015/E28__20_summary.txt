Consequently she will not be able to represent her nation at the coming edition of Miss Universe 2015, slated to be held in Las Vegas, this December 20.

As the beauty queen is unable to complete her duty as Miss Universe Croatia, she will be replaced by her runner up, Mirta Kustan, who will be wearing the sash of Croatia in the scintillating eve of Miss Universe.
We wish Barbara a speedy recovery along with wishing Mirta all the best for her Miss Universe venture!
