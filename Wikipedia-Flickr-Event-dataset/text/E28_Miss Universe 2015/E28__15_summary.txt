The finale of Miss Brazil 2015 held on November 18’ 2015 at the Citibank Hall, concluded with the crowning of Marthina Brandt as the winner.
This was the 61st edition of the Brazil’s most prestigious beauty pageant and the winner, Marthina Brandt, will compete for the Miss Universe 2015 title on December 20 in Las Vegas, Nevada.
Marthina succeeds Melissa Gurgel, Miss Brazil 2014.
