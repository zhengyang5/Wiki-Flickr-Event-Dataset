Paulina Brodd was crowned Miss Universe Sweden 2015 at the end of the pageant held on July, 19 2015.
She will have a few months to prepare for Miss Universe 2015, where she hopes to be crowned by the reigning Miss Universe, Paulina Vega.
Brodd is the daughter of Monica Brodd, Miss Sweden 1992 and TOP 10 in Miss Universe 1992.
The Miss Universe Sweden competes in Miss Universe, The Miss Earth Sweden vies in Miss Earth, and the Miss International Sweden goes to compete in Miss International.
