Use of face masksThe number of people in public places who are displaying symptoms is likely to be limited.
Therefore it should not be necessary for workers to wear facemasks routinely when in contact with the general public.
Assessing the types of control measures needed within the workplaceDH, working with HSE and HPA, has produced guidance for businesses in the eventuality of a flu pandemic.
Further informationThe Department of Health and Health Protection Agency (HPA) are the lead agencies on swine flu.
