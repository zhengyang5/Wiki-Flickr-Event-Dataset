WHO is not recommending travel restrictions related to pandemic (H1N1) 2009.
There is no scientific reason to delay international travel to reduce spread of infection.
Scientific research based on mathematical modelling shows that restricting travel would be of limited or no benefit in stopping the spread of disease.
And again, do not travel if you are ill.(updated from 7 May 2009 and 1 May 2009 versions)
