Interim Guidance for Cruise Ships during the 2009-10 Influenza SeasonThis document provides interim guidance for crew members and passengers of cruise ships originating from or stopping in the United States during the 2009-10 influenza season.
Cruise ship management and medical staff need to be flexible in identifying and caring for persons with influenza.
Medical Evaluation and Management Updated resources for clinicians and guidance on the medical evaluation and management of individuals with 2009 H1N1 influenza.
Facemasks and RespiratorsUse of N95 respirators or facemasks is not generally recommended for cruise ship crew members for general work activities.
Cleaning and DisinfectionRoutine cleaning and disinfection strategies used during influenza seasons can be used for environmental management of 2009 H1N1 influenza.
