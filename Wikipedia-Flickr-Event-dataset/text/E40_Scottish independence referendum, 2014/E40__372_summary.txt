High Level Summary of Statistics Trend Last update: Tuesday, 12 March 2013Scottish Index of Multiple DeprivationThe Scottish Index of Multiple Deprivation (SIMD) identifies small area concentrations of multiple deprivation across all of Scotland.
It allows effective targeting of policies and funding where the aim is to wholly or partly tackle or take account of area concentrations of multiple deprivation.
Key ResultsSIMD 2012 shows that multiple deprivation in Scotland has become less concentrated over time.
The 15% most deprived datazones in Scotland are shared out among local authorities as shown in the ‘National Share’ chart below.
This does not mean there is no deprivation in these areas, just that it is not concentrated in small areas.
