Johann Lamont: 'Redistribution' of wealth 'at the heart' of Labour plans to extend devolution.
Picture: Robert PerryIncreased control of income tax was at the heart of Labour’s package, which also included plans for a “fairer” property tax, devolution of housing benefit and more control over the railways.
Currently, those in the “higher” band earn more than £41,451 and pay a 40 per cent income tax rate.
In addition to the devolution of housing benefit, which would allow future Scottish Governments to abolish the bedroom tax, Labour looked at other aspects of welfare.
Last night, SNP politicians claimed Labour’s proposals represented a “watered down” version of previous models.
