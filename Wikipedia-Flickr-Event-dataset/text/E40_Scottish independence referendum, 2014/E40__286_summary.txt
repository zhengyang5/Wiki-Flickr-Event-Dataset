Image caption Supporters gathered at the Meadows then marched to Princes Street Gardens in the capitalThousands of people have taken part in an event in Edinburgh aimed at demonstrating support for independence.
It was staged under the banner Independence for Scotland and is not part of the official Yes Scotland campaign.
Lothian and Borders Police estimated that about 5,000 people took part in the march and rally.
Margo MacDonald MSPIt is the first in a series of events planned in the run up to the independence vote.
June Sutherland, 64, from Broxburn in West Lothian, said she had not supported independence a year ago but now felt differently.
