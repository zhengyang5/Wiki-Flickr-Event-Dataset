VideoVoters will go to the polls in the Scottish independence referendum on 18 September.
James Cook is in Kelso to host the second in a series of special BBC Scotland debates as the country prepares to make a historic decision on its future.
The programme will be screened between 21:00 and 22:00 on Tuesday on BBC Two Scotland, and will be available for one week after transmission on the BBC iPlayer.
