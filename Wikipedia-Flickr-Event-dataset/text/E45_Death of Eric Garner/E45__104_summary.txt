Getty ImagesKxng Crooked has released a powerful new music video for “I Can’t Breathe,” a track that he released in December as part of his Sex, Money & Hip-Hop album.
"Respect the value of my life, too,” Crooked raps on the track, which was first released exclusively through MTV News.
The clip features footage of protests held after a Staten Island grand jury decided not to indict New York City police officers in the death of local resident Eric Garner.
The "I can't breathe" rallying cries can be heard as the protest footage plays in the clip.
Kxng Crooked's Sex, Money & Hip-Hop album is out now.
