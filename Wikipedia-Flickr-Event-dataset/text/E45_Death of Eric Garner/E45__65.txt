For a second night, protests rippled across the U.S. on Thursday, a day after a grand jury decided not to charge a New York City Police officer over the death of Eric Garner. Photo: AP

More than 300 people have been arrested in New York City during a series of large and unruly protests in the two days following a grand jury’s decision Wednesday to not indict a white police officer in the death of Eric Garner, police said.
