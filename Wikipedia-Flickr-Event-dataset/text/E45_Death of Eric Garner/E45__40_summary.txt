Add InterestEric Garner, who was 6-foot-3 and roughly 350 pounds, died Thursday after police struggled to arrest him for allegedly selling loose cigarettes on Staten Island, according to the NYPD.

Garner was arrested in Staten Island after he was allegedly seen selling "loosie" cigarettes, police said.
