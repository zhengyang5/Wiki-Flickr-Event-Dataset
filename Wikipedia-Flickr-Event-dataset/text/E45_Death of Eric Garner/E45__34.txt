Island Voice Inc. Executive Director Bobby Digi, Retired NYPD Lieutenant Michael Reilly, well-known Criminal Defense Attorney Mario Gallucci, and Criminal Justice Reporter at NY1 Dean Meminger discuss Eric Garner, Staten Island made national headlines this week after a Port Richmond man died while being taken into custody by police in Tompkinsville. Eric Garner died following a confrontation with police who were trying to arrest him for allegedly selling illegal cigarettes. As Garner was laid to rest an investigation into his death continues.
