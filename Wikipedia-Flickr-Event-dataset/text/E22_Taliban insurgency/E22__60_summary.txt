Adjust font size:(CNN) -- Insurgents in southern Afghanistan on Saturday attacked NATO troops, killing two Canadian soldiers and wounding three others, NATO's International Security Assistance Force said.
The militants were wielding small armaments and rocket-propelled grenades when they attacked the troops, NATO said.
The governor of the eastern Laghman province was not hurt but another official was killed, police said, AP reported.
Farther north, ISAF troops arrested insurgents during an operation in the Afghan capital of Kabul on Friday.
The 2,000-plus contingent of Canadian soldiers in Afghanistan is based in Kandahar.
