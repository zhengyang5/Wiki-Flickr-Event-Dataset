By MATTHEW PENNINGTON, Associated Press Writer Tue Feb 20, 1:18 PM ETKABUL, Afghanistan - A suicide bomber dressed in a doctor's white coat slipped into a hospital compound in eastern, where he was challenged by police andsoldiers before detonating his explosives.
Seven NATO troops and a hospital employee were injured, a local official said.
The suicide bomber was initially stopped by Afghan police as he entered the compound of the main government hospital in the city of Khost, where about 150 people had gathered for a ribbon-cutting ceremony to open an emergency ward.
Arsalah Jamal said they were American, and that one hospital employee also was wounded.
Most of the NATO forces in eastern Afghanistan are American.
