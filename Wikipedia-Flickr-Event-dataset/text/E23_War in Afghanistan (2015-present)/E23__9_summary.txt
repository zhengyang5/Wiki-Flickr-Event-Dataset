The Taliban assaulted the northern provincial capital of Kunduz from three directions and seized control of areas in the city.
The Taliban confirmed that it launched a three-pronged assault on Kunduz city.
“Kunduz city is completely with taliban ANSF are out,” Ehsan tweeted.
“[T]he city is completely with taliban now, taliban walking inside streets, i am trapped at home.”Ehsan posted photographs purportedly showing Taliban fighters walking the streets of Kunduz and prisoners who have been freed from the city’s main jail.
Kunduz city would be the first provincial capital to fall to the Taliban.
