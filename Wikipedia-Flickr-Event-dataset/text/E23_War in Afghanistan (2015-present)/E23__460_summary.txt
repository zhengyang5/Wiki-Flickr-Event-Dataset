By David MillerAccording to Tony Blair and George Bush respectively, 'world opinion' and the 'collective will of the world' supported the attack on Afghanistan.
World opinionThe biggest poll of world opinion was carried out by Gallup International in 37 countries in late September (Gallup International 2001).
It is well known that Scottish opinion tends to be to the left of UK opinion, but not by more than a few points on average.
Furthermore there is evidence that dissent in the US is being underrepresented in responses to opinion polls.
