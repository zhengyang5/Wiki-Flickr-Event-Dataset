KUALA LUMPUR: Students in public institutions of higher learning (IPTA) in areas affected by the flood will be allowed to defer their examination, said Second Education Minister Datuk Seri Idris Jusoh.

He advised those who were not able to return to campus to sit for the semester examination, scheduled next week, to contact their respective universities.

"Their vice-chancellors have agreed to defer the examination," he said in his latest Twitter post yesterday.

The IPTA that are located in the flood hit areas are Universiti Malaysia Kelantan, Universiti Malaysia Terengganu and Universiti Sultan Zainal Abidin.
