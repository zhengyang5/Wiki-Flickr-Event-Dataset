Abu Dhabi: Shaikh Hamdan Bin Zayed Al Nahyan, Ruler’s Representative in the Western Region and President of the Emirates Red Crescent (ERC), has ordered an emergency aid be delivered to flood survivors in Malaysia’s north-eastern states of Kelantan, Terengganu and Pahang.

Following Shaikh Hamdan’s directives, a UAE delegation arrived in Kuala Lumpur to lead relief operations in coordination with the UAE Embassy.
