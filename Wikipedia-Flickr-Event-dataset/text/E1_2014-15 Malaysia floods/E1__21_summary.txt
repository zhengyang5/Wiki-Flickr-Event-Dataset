FLOODS Four hospitals in Kelantan and two more in Pahang which were affected by the floods, are still functioning, director-general of health Dr Noor Hisham Abdullah said today.
Noor Hisham said floods that hit Johor, Terengganu, Pahang, Kelantan, Perak and Selangor had affected 102 health facilities, 38 of which were still operating.
